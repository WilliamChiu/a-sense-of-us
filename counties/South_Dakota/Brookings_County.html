Brookings County, South Dakota

In 2000, 6573 people in Brookings County, South Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18823 people in Brookings County, South Dakota lived in family households.

In 2010, 3233 people in Beadle County, South Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 13545 people in Beadle County, South Dakota lived in family households.

The county was founded July 3, 1871, and was named after Wilmot Wood Brookings (1830 - 1905), a politician and pioneer of southeastern South Dakota. Medary was the first county seat for eight years from 1871-1879
