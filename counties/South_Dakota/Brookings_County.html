Brookings County, South Dakota

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 7.1599603%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 18823 people in Brookings County, South Dakota lived in family households.

In 2010, 17398 people in Beadle County, South Dakota lived in the county.
