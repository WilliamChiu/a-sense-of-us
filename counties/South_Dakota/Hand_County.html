Hand County, South Dakota

In 2000, 520 people in Hand County, South Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3151 people in Hand County, South Dakota lived in family households.

In 2010, 829 people in Gregory County, South Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3398 people in Gregory County, South Dakota lived in family households.

Hand County was named for George A. Hand, territorial secretary. It was created in 1873 by the Dakota territorial legislature. The boundaries were finalized in 1882, the year it was organized.
