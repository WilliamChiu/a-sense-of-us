Logan County, Colorado

In 2000, 2897 people in Logan County, Colorado lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15619 people in Logan County, Colorado lived in family households.

In 2010, 65047 people in Larimer County, Colorado lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 226053 people in Larimer County, Colorado lived in family households.

Logan County is one of the 64 counties in the U.S. state of Colorado. As of the 2010 census, the population was 22,709. The county seat is Sterling. The county was named for General John A. Logan.
