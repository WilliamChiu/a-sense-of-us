Rio Blanco County, Colorado

In 2000, 762 people in Rio Blanco County, Colorado lived in nonfamily households.

Rio Blanco County is one of the 64 counties of the U.S. state of Colorado. As of the 2010 census, the population was 6,666. The county seat is Meeker. The name of the county is the Spanish language name for the White River which runs through it.
