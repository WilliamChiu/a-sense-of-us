Huerfano County, Colorado

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 11.542726%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1350 people in Huerfano County, Colorado lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5593 people in Huerfano County, Colorado lived in family households.

In 2000, 919 people in Huerfano County, Colorado lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 7862 people in Huerfano County, Colorado lived in the county.

In 2010, 3291 people in Grand County, Colorado lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11330 people in Grand County, Colorado lived in family households.

In 2010, 222 people in Grand County, Colorado lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 14843 people in Grand County, Colorado lived in the county.

Huerfano County was one of the original 17 counties created by the Territory of Colorado on November 1, 1861, and was originally larger than its present size. On November 2, 1870, the Colorado General Assembly created Greenwood County from former Cheyenne and Arapaho tribal land and the eastern portion of Huerfano County.
