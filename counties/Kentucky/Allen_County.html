Allen County, Kentucky

In 2000, 2060 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15535 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 713 people in Woodson County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2557 people in Woodson County, Kansas lived in family households.

Allen County was established in 1815 from land given by Barren and Warren counties. A courthouse fire in 1902 resulted in the loss of some county records.
