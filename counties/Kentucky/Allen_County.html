Allen County, Kentucky

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.027233362%. This change is fairly different compared to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 2060 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15535 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2000, 205 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 17800 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in the county.

In 2010, 713 people in Woodson County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2557 people in Woodson County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 39 people in Woodson County, Kansas lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 3309 people in Woodson County, Kansas lived in the county.

Allen County was established in 1815 from land given by Barren and Warren counties. A courthouse fire in 1902 resulted in the loss of some county records.
