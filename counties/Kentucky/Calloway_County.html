Calloway County, Kentucky

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 9.10697%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 6840 people in Calloway County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 24341 people in Calloway County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2000, 2996 people in Calloway County, Kentucky lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 34177 people in Calloway County, Kentucky lived in the county.

In 2010, 8089 people in Bullitt County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 65890 people in Bullitt County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 340 people in Bullitt County, Kentucky lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 74319 people in Bullitt County, Kentucky lived in the county.

Calloway County was created in 1822 from land taken from Hickman County. The courthouse was built in 1823. A fire at the courthouse in 1906 caused the almost complete destruction of the county records.
