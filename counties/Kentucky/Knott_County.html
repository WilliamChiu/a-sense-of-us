Knott County, Kentucky

In 2000, 1903 people in Knott County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15173 people in Knott County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 5849 people in Jessamine County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 40958 people in Jessamine County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Knott County was established in 1884 from land given by Breathitt, Floyd, Letcher, and Perry counties. The 1890s-era courthouse, the second to serve the county, burned in 1929.
