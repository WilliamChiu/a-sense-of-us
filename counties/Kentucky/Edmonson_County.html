Edmonson County, Kentucky

In 2000, 1365 people in Edmonson County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10119 people in Edmonson County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 1362 people in Crittenden County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7743 people in Crittenden County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Edmonson County was established on January 12, 1825 from land given by Grayson, Hart and Warren counties. A courthouse built in 1873 replaced a former structure rendered unfit when its floor collapsed.
