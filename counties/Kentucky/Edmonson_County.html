Edmonson County, Kentucky

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.89259744%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 1365 people in Edmonson County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10119 people in Edmonson County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2000, 160 people in Edmonson County, Kentucky lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 11644 people in Edmonson County, Kentucky lived in the county.

In 2010, 1362 people in Crittenden County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7743 people in Crittenden County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 210 people in Crittenden County, Kentucky lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 9315 people in Crittenden County, Kentucky lived in the county.

Edmonson County was established on January 12, 1825 from land given by Grayson, Hart and Warren counties. A courthouse built in 1873 replaced a former structure rendered unfit when its floor collapsed.
