Wolfe County, Kentucky

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.57343245%. This change is fairly different compared to that of the nation. In 2000, 934 people in Wolfe County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5979 people in Wolfe County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2000, 7065 people in Wolfe County, Kentucky lived in the county.

In 2010, 3161 people in Wayne County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20813 people in Wayne County, Kentucky lived in the county.

Wolfe County was formed on March 5, 1860 from portions of Breathitt County, Morgan County, Owsley County and Powell County. It was named for Nathaniel Wolfe, a member of the legislative assembly.
