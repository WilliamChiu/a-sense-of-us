Washington County, Kentucky

In 2000, 1249 people in Washington County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 9332 people in Washington County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 1208 people in Trimble County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7560 people in Trimble County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Washington County was established in 1792 from land given by Nelson County. It was the first county created by the Commonwealth of Kentucky after its separation from Virginia.
