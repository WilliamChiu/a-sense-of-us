Barren County, Kentucky

In 2000, 5020 people in Barren County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 32379 people in Barren County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 2674 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 17085 people in Allen County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Barren County was established in 1798 from land given by Green County and Warren County. Six courthouses have served the county throughout its history, the first built of logs.
