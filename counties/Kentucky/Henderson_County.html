Henderson County, Kentucky

In 2000, 6418 people in Henderson County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 37589 people in Henderson County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 4044 people in Harlan County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 24607 people in Harlan County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Henderson County was established in 1798 from land given by Christian County. The 1843 courthouse was torn down in the 1960s to make way for a new one.
