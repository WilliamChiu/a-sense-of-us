Fleming County, Kentucky

In 2000, 1589 people in Fleming County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12082 people in Fleming County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 934 people in Elliott County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 5857 people in Elliott County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Fleming County was established in 1798 from land given by Mason County. The first courthouse, possibly built of logs, was replaced in 1830 and again in 1952.
