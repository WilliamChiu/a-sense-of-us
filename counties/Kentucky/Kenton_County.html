Kenton County, Kentucky

In 2000, 24266 people in Kenton County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 125332 people in Kenton County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 146508 people in Jefferson County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 580435 people in Jefferson County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Kenton County was established on January 29, 1840, from land given by Campbell County. It was named in honor of Simon Kenton, a pioneer of Kentucky.
