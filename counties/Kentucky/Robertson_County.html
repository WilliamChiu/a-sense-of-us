Robertson County, Kentucky

In 2000, 280 people in Robertson County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1916 people in Robertson County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 9288 people in Pike County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 54636 people in Pike County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Robertson County was formed on February 11, 1867 from portions of Bracken County, Harrison County, Mason County and Nicholas County. It was named after George Robertson, a judge and member of Congress.
