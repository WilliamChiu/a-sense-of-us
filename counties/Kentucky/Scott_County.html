Scott County, Kentucky

In 2000, 3884 people in Scott County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 27709 people in Scott County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 2239 people in Rockcastle County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14476 people in Rockcastle County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Scott County was explored as early as 1774. One of the early settlers was John McClelland of Pennsylvania. The area became subject to hostile Indian attacks, and was abandoned by 1777.
