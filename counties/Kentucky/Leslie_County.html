Leslie County, Kentucky

In 2000, 1361 people in Leslie County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10930 people in Leslie County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 7829 people in Laurel County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 50321 people in Laurel County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Leslie County was founded in 1878. It was named for Preston H. Leslie, Governor of Kentucky (1871-1875).
