Muhlenberg County, Kentucky

In 2000, 3668 people in Muhlenberg County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 26638 people in Muhlenberg County, Kentucky lived in family households.

In 2010, 1726 people in Monroe County, Kentucky lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9094 people in Monroe County, Kentucky lived in family households.

Muhlenberg County was established in 1798 from land given by Logan and Christian counties. Muhlenberg was the 34th Kentucky county in order of formation.
