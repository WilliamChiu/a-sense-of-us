In 2000, 40850 people in Utah County, Utah lived in nonfamily households.

Utah County is a county in the U.S. state of Utah. As of the 2010 census, the population was 516,564, making it the second-most populous county in Utah. The county seat and largest city is Provo, which is the third-largest city in the state. It was named for the Spanish name (''Yuta'') for the Ute Indians.
