Morgan County, Utah

In 2000, 297 people in Morgan County, Utah lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6832 people in Morgan County, Utah lived in family households.

In 2010, 681 people in Juab County, Utah lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9443 people in Juab County, Utah lived in family households.

An early route of the Hastings Cutoff ran through the Morgan Valley and down through a narrow gorge in Weber Canyon. The Donner Party avoided going through the Morgan Valley in order to speed up their journey. However, their alternate route proved more time-consuming.
