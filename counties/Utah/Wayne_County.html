Wayne County, Utah

In 2000, 266 people in Wayne County, Utah lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2236 people in Wayne County, Utah lived in family households.

In 2010, 49704 people in Utah County, Utah lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 452948 people in Utah County, Utah lived in family households.

Wayne County is a county located in the U.S. state of Utah. As of the 2010 census, the population was 2,778. Its county seat is Loa. The county was formed from Piute County in 1892, and gets its name from Wayne County, Tennessee.
