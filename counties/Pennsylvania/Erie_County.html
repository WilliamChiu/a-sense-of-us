Erie County, Pennsylvania

In 2000, 43628 people in Erie County, Pennsylvania lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 223262 people in Erie County, Pennsylvania lived in family households.

In 2010, 50763 people in Dauphin County, Pennsylvania lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 210557 people in Dauphin County, Pennsylvania lived in family households.

Erie County was established on March 12, 1800 from part of Allegheny County, which absorbed the lands of the disputed Erie Triangle in 1792. Prior to 1792, the region was claimed by both New York and Pennsylvania, so no county demarcations were made until the federal government intervened.
