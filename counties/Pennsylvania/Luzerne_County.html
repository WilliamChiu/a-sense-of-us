Luzerne County, Pennsylvania

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 1.6375899%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 53159 people in Luzerne County, Pennsylvania lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 253228 people in Luzerne County, Pennsylvania lived in family households.

In 2000, 12863 people in Luzerne County, Pennsylvania lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 319250 people in Luzerne County, Pennsylvania lived in the county.

In 2010, 14181 people in Lawrence County, Pennsylvania lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 74688 people in Lawrence County, Pennsylvania lived in family households.

In 2010, 2239 people in Lawrence County, Pennsylvania lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 91108 people in Lawrence County, Pennsylvania lived in the county.
