Armstrong County, Pennsylvania

In 2000, 9587 people in Armstrong County, Pennsylvania lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 61630 people in Armstrong County, Pennsylvania lived in family households.

In 2010, 12765 people in Yamhill County, Oregon lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 80967 people in Yamhill County, Oregon lived in family households.

The County was named after John Armstrong, who served as a brigadier general and major general in the Revolutionary War.
