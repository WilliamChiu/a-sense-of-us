Charles County, Maryland

In 2000, 12387 people in Charles County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 106790 people in Charles County, Maryland lived in family households.

In 2010, 4392 people in Caroline County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28232 people in Caroline County, Maryland lived in family households.

Charles County was created in 1658 by an Order in Council. There was an also an earlier Charles County from 1650 to 1653, sometimes referred to in historic documents as Old Charles County.
