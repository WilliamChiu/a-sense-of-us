Washington County, Maryland

In 2000, 18965 people in Washington County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 103538 people in Washington County, Maryland lived in family households.

In 2010, 13541 people in St. Mary's County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 88684 people in St. Mary's County, Maryland lived in family households.

The western portions of Maryland (including present Washington County) were incorporated into Prince George's County in 1696. This original county included six current counties. The first to be created was Frederick, separated from Prince George's County in 1748.
