Prince George's County, Maryland

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 1.3867378%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 116479 people in Prince George's County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 667679 people in Prince George's County, Maryland lived in family households.

In 2000, 17357 people in Prince George's County, Maryland lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 801515 people in Prince George's County, Maryland lived in the county.

In 2010, 35458 people in Howard County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 249305 people in Howard County, Maryland lived in family households.

In 2010, 2322 people in Howard County, Maryland lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 287085 people in Howard County, Maryland lived in the county.
