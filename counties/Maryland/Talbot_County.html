Talbot County, Maryland

In 2000, 5507 people in Talbot County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 27693 people in Talbot County, Maryland lived in family households.

In 2010, 5968 people in Queen Anne's County, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 41404 people in Queen Anne's County, Maryland lived in family households.

The founding date of Talbot County is not known. It existed by February 12, 1661, when a writ was issued to its sheriff. It was initially divided into nine Hundreds and three parishes: St. Paul's, St. Peter's and St. Michael's.
