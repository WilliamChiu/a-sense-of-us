Knox County, Ohio

In 2000, 6681 people in Knox County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 44357 people in Knox County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 8106 people in Huron County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 50942 people in Huron County, Ohio lived in family households.

Knox County was formed from Fairfield County in 1808. A rich African American cultural heritage has existed in the county since its founding.
