Jefferson County, Ohio

In 2000, 11329 people in Jefferson County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 60461 people in Jefferson County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 2968 people in Holmes County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 38633 people in Holmes County, Ohio lived in family households.

Jefferson County was organized on July 29, 1797 by proclamation of Governor Arthur St. Clair, six years before Ohio was granted statehood.
