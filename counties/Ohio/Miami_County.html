In 2000, 12453 people in Miami County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

Miami County is a county located in the U.S. state of Ohio. As of the 2010 census, the population was 102,506. Its county seat is Troy. The county is named for an Indigenous American word of disputed meaning.
