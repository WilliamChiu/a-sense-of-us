Morgan County, Ohio

In 2000, 1971 people in Morgan County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12741 people in Morgan County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 14644 people in Miami County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 86807 people in Miami County, Ohio lived in family households.

Morgan County was formed on December 29, 1817, from portions of Guernsey, Muskingum and Washington Counties. It was named after Daniel Morgan, a member in the Congress for Virginia, and general in the American Revolutionary War.
