Belmont County, Ohio

In 2000, 10174 people in Belmont County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 56904 people in Belmont County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 15714 people in Ashtabula County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 82593 people in Ashtabula County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 1976, Belmont County became the first American county to elect a female sheriff, Katherine Crumbly.
