Gallia County, Ohio

In 2000, 3996 people in Gallia County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 26128 people in Gallia County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 4426 people in Fayette County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 24011 people in Fayette County, Ohio lived in family households.

Gallia County was formed on March 25, 1803 from portions of Adams and Washington counties. Gallia County was originally settled by the French who gave the county the Latin name for "France."
