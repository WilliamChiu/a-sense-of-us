Guernsey County, Ohio

In 2000, 5712 people in Guernsey County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 34573 people in Guernsey County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 4464 people in Gallia County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 25549 people in Gallia County, Ohio lived in family households.

Guernsey County, located in the Appalachian foothills, was first formed and organized in 1810 from portions of Muskingum and Belmont counties. It lost some land area during the formation of neighboring counties until it reached its present boundaries in 1851.
