Carroll County, Ohio

In 2000, 3492 people in Carroll County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 24978 people in Carroll County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 11515 people in Belmont County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 55051 people in Belmont County, Ohio lived in family households.

Carroll County was formed on December 25, 1833 from portions of Columbiana, Harrison, Jefferson, Stark, and Tuscarawas counties.
