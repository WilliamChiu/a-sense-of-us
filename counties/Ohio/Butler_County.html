Butler County, Ohio

In 2000, 46153 people in Butler County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 275407 people in Butler County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 6064 people in Auglaize County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 39360 people in Auglaize County, Ohio lived in family households.

The county was once home to seven large earthworks sites, built by ancient Indigenous peoples of the Americas that lived in the area.
