Cuyahoga County, Ohio

In 2000, 252840 people in Cuyahoga County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1110960 people in Cuyahoga County, Ohio lived in family households.

In 2010, 16197 people in Columbiana County, Ohio lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 87700 people in Columbiana County, Ohio lived in family households.

Cuyahoga County was created on June 7, 1807 and organized on May 1, 1810. It was later reduced by the creation of Huron, Lake, and Lorain Counties. It was named after the Cuyahoga River.
