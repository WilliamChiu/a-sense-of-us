Hamilton County, New York

In 2000, 927 people in Hamilton County, New York lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 4357 people in Hamilton County, New York lived in family households.

In 2010, 10327 people in Fulton County, New York lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 43730 people in Fulton County, New York lived in family households.

On April 12, 1816, Hamilton County was created by partitioning 1800 sqmi from Montgomery County, but due to low population it remained unorganized and administered from Montgomery County, N.Y. until it was recognized as sufficiently organized for self-government on January 1, 1838. The organization process was completed by Summer of 1847.
