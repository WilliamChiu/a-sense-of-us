Greene County, New York

In 2000, 7480 people in Greene County, New York lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 36772 people in Greene County, New York lived in family households.

On March 25, 1800, Greene County was created by the partitioning of Albany (360 Square Miles) and Ulster (270 Square Miles) counties, producing a county of 620 sqmi.
