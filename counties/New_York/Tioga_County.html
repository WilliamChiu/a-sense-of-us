Tioga County, New York

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.61846375%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 6636 people in Tioga County, New York lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 44637 people in Tioga County, New York lived in family households.

In 2000, 511 people in Tioga County, New York lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 51784 people in Tioga County, New York lived in the county.

In 2010, 1583 people in Steuben County, New York lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 98990 people in Steuben County, New York lived in the county.
