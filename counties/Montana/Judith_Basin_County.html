Judith Basin County, Montana

In 2000, 318 people in Judith Basin County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2011 people in Judith Basin County, Montana lived in family households.

In 2010, 606 people in Granite County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2433 people in Granite County, Montana lived in family households.

Judith Basin County was formed from western Fergus County, Montana and eastern Cascade County, Montana on December 10, 1920. In 1895, Yogo sapphires were discovered at Yogo Gulch, about 15 miles southwest of Utica, which at the time was in Fergus County.
