Beaverhead County, Montana

In 2000, 1637 people in Beaverhead County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 7071 people in Beaverhead County, Montana lived in family households.

In 2010, 366 people in Worth County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1753 people in Worth County, Missouri lived in family households.

The county name is derived from a rock formation, which the Shoshone described as being shaped like a beaver's head.
