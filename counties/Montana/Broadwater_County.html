Broadwater County, Montana

In 2000, 546 people in Broadwater County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3779 people in Broadwater County, Montana lived in family households.

In 2010, 2041 people in Beaverhead County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6761 people in Beaverhead County, Montana lived in family households.

The Lewis and Clark Expedition travelled through what is now Broadwater County as they traced the Missouri River. Gold was discovered in the Big Belt Mountains in 1864 which brought several mining towns. In 1881 the Northern Pacific Railway with a stop in what is now Townsend.
