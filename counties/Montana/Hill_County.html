Hill County, Montana

In 2000, 2666 people in Hill County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13663 people in Hill County, Montana lived in family households.

In 2010, 1489 people in Glacier County, Montana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11220 people in Glacier County, Montana lived in family households.

The first European-American settlement was Fort Assinniboine, first garrisoned by the United States Army in 1879. Fifteen of the original 104 structures from the fort are still standing.
