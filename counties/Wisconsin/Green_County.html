Green County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 4876 people in Green County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 28263 people in Green County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

In 2010, 1553 people in Forest County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7372 people in Forest County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

Green County is named for Gen. Nathanael Greene.
