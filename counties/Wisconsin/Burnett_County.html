Burnett County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 2519 people in Burnett County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12895 people in Burnett County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

In 2010, 43729 people in Brown County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 197649 people in Brown County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

Burnett County is a county located in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. As of the 2010 census, the population was 15,457. Its county seat is Meenon with the majority of county governmental services located at the Burnett County Government Center.
