Washington County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 13817 people in Washington County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

Washington County is a county in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. It is named after President George Washington. As of the 2010 census, the population was 131,887. Its county seat is West Bend.
