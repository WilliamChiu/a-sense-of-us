Washburn County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 2459 people in Washburn County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

Washburn County is a county in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. It is named after Governor Cadwallader C. Washburn. As of the 2010 census, the population was 15,911. Its county seat is Shell Lake.
