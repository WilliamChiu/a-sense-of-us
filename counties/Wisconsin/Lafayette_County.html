Lafayette County, sometimes spelled La Fayette County, is a county located in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. As of the 2010 census, the population was 16,836. Its county seat is Darlington. The county was named in honor of the Marquis de Lafayette, the French general who rendered assistance to the Continental Army in the American Revolutionary War.
