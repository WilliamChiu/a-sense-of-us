Bayfield County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 2288 people in Bayfield County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12605 people in Bayfield County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

In 2010, 3230 people in Ashland County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12304 people in Ashland County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

Originally, in 1848 it was named La Pointe County, Wisconsin. After Douglas (1854) and Ashland (1860) Counties were split off from the original La Pointe County, the remainder was renamed Bayfield County on 12 April 1866.
