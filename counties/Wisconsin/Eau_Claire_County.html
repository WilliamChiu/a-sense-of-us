Eau Claire County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 19457 people in Eau Claire County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 68580 people in Eau Claire County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

In 2010, 8949 people in Douglas County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 33838 people in Douglas County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

Eau Claire county was originally set off as the '''Town of Clearwater''' in Chippewa County, in 1855. The name was changed to the Town of Eau Claire on March 31, 1856. The entire town was separated as Eau Claire County by an act of the Wisconsin State Legislature on October 6, 1856.
