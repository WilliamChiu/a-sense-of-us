Clark County, Wisconsin

In 2000, 4009 people in Clark County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 28925 people in Clark County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

In 2010, 5853 people in Calumet County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 42920 people in Calumet County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

Clark County was named for A. W. Clark, an early settler, or for General George Rogers Clark.
