Kauai County, Hawaii

In 2000, 7252 people in Kauai County, Hawaii lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 50579 people in Kauai County, Hawaii lived in family households.

In 2010, 30767 people in Hawaii County, Hawaii lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 150668 people in Hawaii County, Hawaii lived in family households.

Kauai County is a county located in the U.S. state of Hawaii. It consists of the islands of Kauai, Niihau, Lehua, and Kaula. As of the 2010 Census the population was 67,091. The county seat is Līhue.
