Thomas County, Kansas

In 2000, 1335 people in Thomas County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6564 people in Thomas County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 265 people in Stanton County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1923 people in Stanton County, Kansas lived in family households.

The county was founded in October 8, 1885. It was named for George Henry Thomas, a Union General during the American Civil War, hero of the Battle of Chickamauga. The townships of the county were named after the soldiers that died at the Battle of Chickamauga.
