Graham County, Kansas

In 2000, 456 people in Graham County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2421 people in Graham County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 3784 people in Franklin County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 21730 people in Franklin County, Kansas lived in family households.

Graham County was organized in 1880. It is named for Captain John L. Graham, a Union soldier killed in action at the Battle of Chickamauga in Tennessee on September 19, 1863.
