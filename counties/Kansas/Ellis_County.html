Ellis County, Kansas

In 2000, 5889 people in Ellis County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 20359 people in Ellis County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 31417 people in Douglas County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 70617 people in Douglas County, Kansas lived in family households.

Ellis County was founded on February 26, 1867. It is named for George Ellis, first lieutenant of the Twelfth Kansas Infantry.
