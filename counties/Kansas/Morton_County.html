Morton County, Kansas

In 2000, 377 people in Morton County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3062 people in Morton County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 1245 people in Mitchell County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4882 people in Mitchell County, Kansas lived in family households.

Morton County was named for Oliver Morton, who was a United States Senator from Indiana from 1867 to 1877.
