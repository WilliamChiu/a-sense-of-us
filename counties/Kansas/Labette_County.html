Labette County, Kansas

In 2000, 3517 people in Labette County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18461 people in Labette County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 389 people in Kearny County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3507 people in Kearny County, Kansas lived in family households.

In the 1840s Labette County had a population mainly consisting of Osage people, although there were also many Cherokee and Euro-Americans. Many of the Euro-Americans were merchants with Osage or Cherokee wives.
