Haskell County, Kansas

In 2000, 362 people in Haskell County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3910 people in Haskell County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 411 people in Hamilton County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2279 people in Hamilton County, Kansas lived in family households.

Haskell County was founded in 1887. It was named for Dudley C. Haskell, a former member of Congress.
