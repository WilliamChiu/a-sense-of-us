Reno County, Kansas

In 2000, 9540 people in Reno County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 51997 people in Reno County, Kansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 2691 people in Pottawatomie County, Kansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 18614 people in Pottawatomie County, Kansas lived in family households.

For millennia, the land now known as Kansas was inhabited by Native Americans. In 1803, most of modern Kansas was secured by the United States as part of the Louisiana Purchase. In 1854, the Kansas Territory was organized, then in 1861 Kansas became the 34th U.S. state. In 1867, '''Reno County''' was founded.
