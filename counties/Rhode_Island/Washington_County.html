Washington County, Rhode Island

In 2000, 19977 people in Washington County, Rhode Island lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 98324 people in Washington County, Rhode Island lived in family households.

In 2010, 30566 people in Kent County, Rhode Island lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 134056 people in Kent County, Rhode Island lived in family households.

Washington County was created from Providence Plantations in 1729 as Kings County. It was renamed Washington County on October 29, 1781, in honor of General and President Washington.
