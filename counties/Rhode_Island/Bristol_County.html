Bristol County, Rhode Island

In 2000, 6817 people in Bristol County, Rhode Island lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 41057 people in Bristol County, Rhode Island lived in family households.

In 2010, 60301 people in Westmoreland County, Pennsylvania lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 296906 people in Westmoreland County, Pennsylvania lived in family households.

The county was formed by the transfer of part of Bristol County, Massachusetts, to the state of Rhode Island, and was the subject of a long-running border dispute.
