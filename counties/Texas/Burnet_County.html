Burnet County, Texas

In 2000, 4145 people in Burnet County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 29110 people in Burnet County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 856 people in Brooks County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6300 people in Brooks County, Texas lived in family households.

Indigenous peoples inhabit the area as early as 4500 B.C. Later known tribes in the area include Tonkawa, Lipan Apache and Comanche.
