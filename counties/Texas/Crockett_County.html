Crockett County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -14.026552%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 443 people in Crockett County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3601 people in Crockett County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2000, 55 people in Crockett County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 4099 people in Crockett County, Texas lived in the county.

In 2010, 6763 people in Coryell County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 57181 people in Coryell County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 11444 people in Coryell County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 75388 people in Coryell County, Texas lived in the county.
