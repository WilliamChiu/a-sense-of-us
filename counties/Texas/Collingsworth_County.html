Collingsworth County, Texas

In 2000, 400 people in Collingsworth County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2753 people in Collingsworth County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 538 people in Coke County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2747 people in Coke County, Texas lived in family households.

The county was created in 1876 from the Bexar and Young land district of Texas. Collingsworth County was organized in 1890 with Wellington as the county seat. From 1883 until 1896 the county was home to the English owned Rocking Chair Ranche.
