Coryell County, Texas

In 2000, 5250 people in Coryell County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 52796 people in Coryell County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 1990 people in Comanche County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11823 people in Comanche County, Texas lived in family households.

The habitation of Coryell County dates as far back as 4500 BC. The Tonkawa, Lipan Apache, Kiowa and Comanche were among the tribes who migrated through the area at various periods.
