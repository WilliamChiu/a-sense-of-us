In 2000, 3924 people in Howard County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

Howard County is a county located in the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census, its population was 35,012. Its county seat is Big Spring. The county is named for Volney E. Howard, a U.S. Congressman from Texas.
