Lampasas County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.80267787%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 357 people in Lampasas County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 17762 people in Lampasas County, Texas lived in the county.
