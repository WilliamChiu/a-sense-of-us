Nolan County, Texas

In 2000, 2159 people in Nolan County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13143 people in Nolan County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 11435 people in Nacogdoches County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 47945 people in Nacogdoches County, Texas lived in family households.

Nolan County is a county located in the west central region of the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census, its population was 15,216. Its county seat is Sweetwater. The county is named for Philip Nolan, one of the first American traders to visit Texas.
