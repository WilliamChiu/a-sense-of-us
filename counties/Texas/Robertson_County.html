In 2000, 2024 people in Robertson County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

Robertson County is a county located in the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census, its population was 16,622. Its county seat is Franklin. The county is named for Sterling C. Robertson, an early settler who signed the Texas Declaration of Independence.
