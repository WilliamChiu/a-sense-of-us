Webb County, Texas

In 2000, 8671 people in Webb County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 181368 people in Webb County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 5344 people in Waller County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 34158 people in Waller County, Texas lived in family households.

Webb County was split in 1856. Encinal County was established on February 1, 1856 and was to have consisted of the eastern portion of Webb County. However, Encinal County was never organized and was finally dissolved on March 12, 1899, and the territory in question returned to Webb County.
