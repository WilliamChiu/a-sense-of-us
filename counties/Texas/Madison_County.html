Madison County, Texas

In 2000, 1248 people in Madison County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8821 people in Madison County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 1211 people in McCulloch County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6963 people in McCulloch County, Texas lived in family households.

The current Madison County Courthouse was built in 1970. It is at least the fifth courthouse to serve Madison County.
