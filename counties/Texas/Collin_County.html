Collin County, Texas

In 2000, 61604 people in Collin County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 426739 people in Collin County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 342 people in Cochran County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2707 people in Cochran County, Texas lived in family households.

Both the county and the county seat were named after Collin McKinney (1766-1861), one of the five men who drafted the Texas Declaration of Independence and the oldest of the 59 men who signed it.
