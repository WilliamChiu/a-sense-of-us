El Paso County, Texas

In 2000, 52144 people in El Paso County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 614734 people in El Paso County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 16664 people in Ector County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 118291 people in Ector County, Texas lived in family households.

El Paso County is the westernmost county in Texas. As of the 2010 census, the population was 800,647, making it the sixth-most populous county in Texas. Its county seat is El Paso, the sixth-most populous city in Texas and the 19th-most populous city in the United States.
