Colorado County, Texas

In 2000, 2530 people in Colorado County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1511 people in Coleman County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

Colorado County is a county located in the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census, its population was 20,874. Its county seat is Columbus. The county was founded in 1826 and is named for the Colorado River of Texas.
