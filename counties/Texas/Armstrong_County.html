Armstrong County, Texas

In 2000, 215 people in Armstrong County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1856 people in Armstrong County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 10244 people in Angelina County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 73574 people in Angelina County, Texas lived in family households.

Paleo-Indians first inhabitants as far back as 10,000 BC. Apachean cultures roamed the county until Comanche dominated around 1700. The Comanches were defeated by the United States Army in the Red River War of 1874. Later tribes include Kiowa, Cheyenne.
