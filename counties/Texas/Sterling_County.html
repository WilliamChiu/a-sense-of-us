Sterling County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -1.6147375%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 136 people in Sterling County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1232 people in Sterling County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2000, 25 people in Sterling County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 1393 people in Sterling County, Texas lived in the county.
