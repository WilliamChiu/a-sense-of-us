Rockwall County, Texas

In 2000, 3178 people in Rockwall County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 39285 people in Rockwall County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 951 people in Refugio County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6303 people in Refugio County, Texas lived in family households.

Rockwall County was formed in 1873 from portions of Kaufman County. It split off because access to the county seat of Kaufman was inconvenient. It was named for its county seat, Rockwall.
