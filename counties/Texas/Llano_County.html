Llano County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -5.4320574%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 2861 people in Llano County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13935 people in Llano County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2000, 248 people in Llano County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 17044 people in Llano County, Texas lived in the county.
