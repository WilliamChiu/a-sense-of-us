Hood County, Texas

In 2000, 4896 people in Hood County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 35603 people in Hood County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 45387 people in Hidalgo County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 722400 people in Hidalgo County, Texas lived in family households.

Hood County was formed in 1866 from portions of Johnson County. It was named after John Bell Hood, a general of the Confederate Army and commander of Hood's Texas Brigade.
