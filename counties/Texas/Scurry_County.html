Scurry County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 10.138929%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1785 people in Scurry County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12914 people in Scurry County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2000, 1662 people in Scurry County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 16361 people in Scurry County, Texas lived in the county.

In 2010, 680 people in San Patricio County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 64804 people in San Patricio County, Texas lived in the county.
