In 2000, 1718 people in Moore County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

Moore County is a county located in the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census, its population was 21,904. The county seat is Dumas. The county is named for Edwin Ward Moore, the commander of the Texas Navy.
