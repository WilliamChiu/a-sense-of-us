Harris County, Texas

In 2000, 461016 people in Harris County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2897428 people in Harris County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 554 people in Hansford County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4999 people in Hansford County, Texas lived in family households.

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 1777 sqmi, of which 1704 sqmi is land and 73 sqmi (4.1%) is water. Both its total area and land area are larger than the state of Rhode Island.
