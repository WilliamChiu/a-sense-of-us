Jackson County, Texas

In 2000, 1654 people in Jackson County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3132 people in Hutchinson County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

Jackson County is a county located in the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census its population was 14,075. Its county seat is Edna. The county is named for Andrew Jackson, President of the United States from 1829 to 1837.
