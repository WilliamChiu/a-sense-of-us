Carson County, Texas

In 2000, 629 people in Carson County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5788 people in Carson County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 1825 people in Callahan County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11644 people in Callahan County, Texas lived in family households.

Prehistoric hunter-gatherer were the first inhabitants, followed by the Plains Apache. Modern Apache tribes followed them and were displaced by Comanches. The Comanches were defeated by the United States Army in the Red River War of 1874.
