Guadalupe County, Texas

In 2000, 8681 people in Guadalupe County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 78730 people in Guadalupe County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 17395 people in Grayson County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 101268 people in Grayson County, Texas lived in family households.

Indigenous paleo-Indian hunter-gatherers were the first inhabitants of the area, thousands of years before European colonization. Later historic Indian tribes settled in the area, including Tonkawa, Karankawa, Kickapoo, Lipan Apache, and Comanche.
