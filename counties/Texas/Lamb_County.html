Lamb County, Texas

In 2000, 1509 people in Lamb County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12913 people in Lamb County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 5049 people in Kleberg County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 25038 people in Kleberg County, Texas lived in family households.

Lamb County was formed in 1876 from portions of Bexar County. It was named after George A. Lamb, a soldier in the Battle of San Jacinto.
