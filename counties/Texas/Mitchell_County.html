Mitchell County, Texas

In 2000, 925 people in Mitchell County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

Mitchell County is a county in the U.S. state of Texas. As of the 2010 census, its population was 9,403. Its county seat is Colorado City. The county is named for Asa Mitchell and Eli Mitchell, two early settlers and soldiers in the Texas Revolution.
