Gonzales County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.38596392%. This change is reasionably similar to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2189 people in Gonzales County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 16076 people in Gonzales County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2000, 363 people in Gonzales County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 18628 people in Gonzales County, Texas lived in the county.

In 2010, 3749 people in Gillespie County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20698 people in Gillespie County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 390 people in Gillespie County, Texas lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 24837 people in Gillespie County, Texas lived in the county.
