Fayette County, Texas

In 2000, 3005 people in Fayette County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18239 people in Fayette County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 7782 people in Erath County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28220 people in Erath County, Texas lived in family households.

Fayette County was established in 1837 from land given by Bastrop and Colorado counties. It is named for the Marquis de la Fayette, a French nobleman who became an American Revolutionary War hero.
