Smith County, Texas

In 2000, 22062 people in Smith County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 147970 people in Smith County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 483 people in Shackelford County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2859 people in Shackelford County, Texas lived in family households.
