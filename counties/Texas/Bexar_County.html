Bexar County, Texas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.75170994%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. In 2000, 1392931 people in Bexar County, Texas lived in the county.

In 2010, 3726 people in Baylor County, Texas lived in the county.

Bexar County was created on December 20, 1836, and encompassed almost the entire western portion of the Republic of Texas. This included the disputed areas of western New Mexico northward to Wyoming. After statehood, 128 counties were carved out of its area.
