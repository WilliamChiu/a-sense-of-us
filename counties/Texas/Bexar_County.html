Bexar County, Texas

In 2000, 176602 people in Bexar County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1181818 people in Bexar County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 694 people in Baylor County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2967 people in Baylor County, Texas lived in family households.

Bexar County was created on December 20, 1836, and encompassed almost the entire western portion of the Republic of Texas. This included the disputed areas of western New Mexico northward to Wyoming. After statehood, 128 counties were carved out of its area.
