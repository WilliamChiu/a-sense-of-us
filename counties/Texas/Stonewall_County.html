Stonewall County, Texas

In 2000, 237 people in Stonewall County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1415 people in Stonewall County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 2795 people in Starr County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 57363 people in Starr County, Texas lived in family households.

Stonewall County was formed in 1876 from portions of Bexar County and Young County. It was named after Thomas Jonathan "Stonewall" Jackson, a general of the Confederate Army.
