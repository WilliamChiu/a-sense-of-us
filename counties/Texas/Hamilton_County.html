Hamilton County, Texas

In 2000, 1176 people in Hamilton County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6818 people in Hamilton County, Texas lived in family households.

In 2010, 13358 people in Guadalupe County, Texas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 116287 people in Guadalupe County, Texas lived in family households.

Indigenous peoples were the first inhabitants of the area. Later Indian tribes settled in the area, including Tawakoni, Tonkawa, Waco and Comanche.
