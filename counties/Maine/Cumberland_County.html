Cumberland County, Maine

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -8.522338%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 52202 people in Cumberland County, Maine lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 204783 people in Cumberland County, Maine lived in family households.

In 2000, 8627 people in Cumberland County, Maine lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 265612 people in Cumberland County, Maine lived in the county.
