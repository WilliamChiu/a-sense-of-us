Oxford County is a county located in the U.S. state of Maine. As of the 2010 Census, the county had a population of 57,833. Its county seat is Paris. The county was formed on March 4, 1805 from northerly portions of York and Cumberland counties. Its Canadian border is the province of Quebec.
