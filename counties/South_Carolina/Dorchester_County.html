Dorchester County, South Carolina

In 2000, 10190 people in Dorchester County, South Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 84134 people in Dorchester County, South Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 5452 people in Colleton County, South Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 33052 people in Colleton County, South Carolina lived in family households.

Dorchester County is named for its first settlement area, which was established by Congregationalists in 1696. These settlers applied the name "Dorchester" after their last residence in Dorchester, Massachusetts.
