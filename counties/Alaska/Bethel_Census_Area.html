Bethel Census Area, Alaska

In 2000, 1305 people in Bethel Census Area, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14460 people in Bethel Census Area, Alaska lived in family households.

In 2010, 1572 people in Bethel Census Area, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 15103 people in Bethel Census Area, Alaska lived in family households.

Bethel Census Area is a census area located in the U.S. state of Alaska. As of the 2010 census, the population is 17,013. It is part of the unorganized borough and therefore has no borough seat. Its largest community by far is the city of Bethel, which is also the largest city in the entire Unorganized Borough.
