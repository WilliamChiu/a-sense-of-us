From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -7.257664%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2010, 2543 people in Aleutians West Census Area, Alaska lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 5561 people in Aleutians West Census Area, Alaska lived in the county.

Aleutians West Census Area is a census area located in the U.S. state of Alaska. As of the 2010 census, the population was 5,561. It is part of the Unorganized Borough and therefore has no borough seat. Its largest city is Unalaska. It contains most of the Aleutian Islands, from Attu Island in the west to Unalaska Island in the east, as well as the Pribilof Islands, which lie north of the Aleutians in the Bering Sea.
