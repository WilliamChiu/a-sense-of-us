Denali Borough, Alaska

In 2000, 391 people in Denali Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1398 people in Denali Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

In 2010, 426 people in Denali Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1364 people in Denali Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

The Denali Borough is a borough located in the U.S. state of Alaska. As of 2010 the population of the borough was 1,826. The borough seat is Healy, and its only incorporated place is Anderson. The borough was incorporated in 1990.
