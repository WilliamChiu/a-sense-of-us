Southeast Fairbanks Census Area, Alaska

In 2000, 703 people in Southeast Fairbanks Census Area, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5172 people in Southeast Fairbanks Census Area, Alaska lived in family households.

In 2010, 1711 people in Sitka City and Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6915 people in Sitka City and Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

Southeast Fairbanks Census Area is a census area located in the U.S. state of Alaska. As of the 2010 census, the population was 7,029. It is part of the unorganized borough and therefore has no borough seat. Its largest communities are Deltana and Tok, both unincorporated CDPs.
