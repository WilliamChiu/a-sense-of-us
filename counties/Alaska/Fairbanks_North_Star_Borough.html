Fairbanks North Star Borough, Alaska

In 2000, 12195 people in Fairbanks North Star Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 67565 people in Fairbanks North Star Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

In 2010, 16499 people in Fairbanks North Star Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 76769 people in Fairbanks North Star Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

The Fairbanks North Star Borough is a borough located in the state of Alaska. As of the 2010 census, the population was 97,581. The borough seat is Fairbanks. The borough's land area is slightly smaller than that of the state of New Jersey.
