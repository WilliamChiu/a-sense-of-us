Haines Borough, Alaska

In 2000, 421 people in Haines Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1966 people in Haines Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

In 2010, 580 people in Haines Borough, Alaska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1928 people in Haines Borough, Alaska lived in family households.

Haines Borough is a home-rule borough located in the state of Alaska. As of the 2010 census, the population was 2,508.
