Talladega County, Alabama

In 2000, 9786 people in Talladega County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 66834 people in Talladega County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 11230 people in Talladega County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 67918 people in Talladega County, Alabama lived in family households.

Prior to Euro-American settlement in what is today Talladega County it was the home of the Abihka tribe of the Creek Confederacy.
