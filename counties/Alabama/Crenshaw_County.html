Crenshaw County, Alabama

In 2000, 1820 people in Crenshaw County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11685 people in Crenshaw County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 2012 people in Crenshaw County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11762 people in Crenshaw County, Alabama lived in family households.

Crenshaw County was established on November 30, 1866. It was formed from parts of Butler, Coffee, Covington, Pike and Lowndes counties.
