Chambers County, Alabama

In 2000, 4834 people in Chambers County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 31231 people in Chambers County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 5152 people in Chambers County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28605 people in Chambers County, Alabama lived in family households.

Prior to contact with people of European descent, what is now Chambers County was inhabited by the Creek nation.
