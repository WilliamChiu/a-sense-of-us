Tallapoosa County, Alabama

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.7015705%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 5387 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 35229 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2000, 859 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 41475 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in the county.

In 2010, 5981 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 35059 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 576 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 41616 people in Tallapoosa County, Alabama lived in the county.

Tallapoosa County was established on December 18, 1832.
