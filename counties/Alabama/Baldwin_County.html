Baldwin County, Alabama

In 2000, 17757 people in Baldwin County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 120384 people in Baldwin County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 26604 people in Baldwin County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 153354 people in Baldwin County, Alabama lived in family households.

Baldwin County was established on December 21, 1809, ten years before Alabama became a state. Previously, the county had been a part of the Mississippi Territory until 1817, when the area was included in the separate Alabama Territory. Statehood was gained by Alabama in 1819.
