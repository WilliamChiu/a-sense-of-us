Fayette County, Alabama

In 2000, 2362 people in Fayette County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15793 people in Fayette County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 2453 people in Fayette County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14497 people in Fayette County, Alabama lived in family households.

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 629 sqmi, of which 628 sqmi is land and 1.7 sqmi (0.3%) is water.
