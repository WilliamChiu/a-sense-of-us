Coffee County, Alabama

In 2000, 5685 people in Coffee County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 37150 people in Coffee County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 7297 people in Coffee County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 42051 people in Coffee County, Alabama lived in family households.

The land in Coffee County was originally part of Dale County, which was incorporated in 1824. Coffee County was formed from the western part of Dale County on December 29, 1841.
