St. Clair County, Alabama

In 2000, 6546 people in St. Clair County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 56293 people in St. Clair County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 9688 people in St. Clair County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 71938 people in St. Clair County, Alabama lived in family households.

St. Clair County was established on November 20, 1818 by the Alabama Territory legislature by splitting the area from Shelby County.
