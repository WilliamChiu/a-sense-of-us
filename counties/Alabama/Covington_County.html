Covington County, Alabama

In 2000, 5314 people in Covington County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 31794 people in Covington County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 5760 people in Covington County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 31430 people in Covington County, Alabama lived in family households.

Covington County was established on December 17, 1821. The Alabama state legislature changed the name to Jones County on August 6, 1868. Two months later on October 10, 1868, the original name was restored.
