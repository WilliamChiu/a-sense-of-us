Coosa County, Alabama

In 2000, 1454 people in Coosa County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10356 people in Coosa County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 1678 people in Coosa County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9737 people in Coosa County, Alabama lived in family households.

The county was established on December 18, 1832, formed from parts of Montgomery and Shelby counties. It gained a small snippet from Montgomery County in 1837, and lost a portion to the south upon the creation of Elmore County.
