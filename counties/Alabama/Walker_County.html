Walker County, Alabama

In 2000, 8785 people in Walker County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 60907 people in Walker County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 9079 people in Walker County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 57078 people in Walker County, Alabama lived in family households.

Walker County was established on December 26, 1823, and formed from sections of Marion and Tuscaloosa counties.
