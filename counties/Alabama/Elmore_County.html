Household Populations

Elmore County, Alabama

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 1.2992501%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 5981 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 54552 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2000, 5341 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 65874 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in the county.

In 2010, 8593 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 65227 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 5483 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 79303 people in Elmore County, Alabama lived in the county.

Over the past two decades, Elmore County has transferred from an economy based on agriculture to one of Alabama's fastest growing counties. According to a recent report, 1110 jobs were created over the last 4 years.
