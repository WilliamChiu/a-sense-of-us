Houston County, Alabama

In 2000, 12310 people in Houston County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 75329 people in Houston County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 15116 people in Houston County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 85015 people in Houston County, Alabama lived in family households.

Houston County was established on February 9, 1903, from parts of Dale County, Geneva County and Henry County. It was named after George Smith Houston, the 24th Governor of Alabama.
