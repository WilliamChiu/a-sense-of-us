Greene County, Alabama

In 2000, 1364 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8532 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 1497 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7503 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in family households.

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 660 sqmi, of which 647 sqmi is land and 13 sqmi (1.9%) is water.
