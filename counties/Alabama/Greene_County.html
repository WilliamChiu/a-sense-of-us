Greene County, Alabama

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.28675795%. This change is reasionably similar to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1364 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8532 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2000, 78 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 9974 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in the county.

In 2010, 1497 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7503 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 45 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 9045 people in Greene County, Alabama lived in the county.

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 660 sqmi, of which 647 sqmi is land and 13 sqmi (1.9%) is water.
