Franklin County, Alabama

In 2000, 3777 people in Franklin County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 27044 people in Franklin County, Alabama lived in family households.

In 2010, 4027 people in Franklin County, Alabama lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 27405 people in Franklin County, Alabama lived in family households.

Colbert County was originally established on February 6, 1867 after it split from Franklin County over political issues after the American Civil War. It was abolished eight months later by an Alabama constitutional convention and then reestablished on February 24, 1870.
