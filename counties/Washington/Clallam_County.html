Clallam County is a county in the U.S. state of Washington. As of the 2010 census, the population was 71,404. The county seat and largest city is Port Angeles. The name is a Klallam word for "the strong people." The county was formed on April 26, 1854. It is south from the Canadian border of British Columbia.
