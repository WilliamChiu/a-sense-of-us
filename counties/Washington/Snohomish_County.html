Snohomish County, Washington

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.11609793%. This change is reasionably similar to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 87963 people in Snohomish County, Washington lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 508949 people in Snohomish County, Washington lived in family households.

In 2000, 9112 people in Snohomish County, Washington lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 606024 people in Snohomish County, Washington lived in the county.
