Hendricks County, Indiana

In 2000, 9945 people in Hendricks County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 90930 people in Hendricks County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 30597 people in Hamilton County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 242344 people in Hamilton County, Indiana lived in family households.

Hendricks County was formed in 1824. It was is named for Indiana Governor William Hendricks, who was serving at the time the County was formed.
