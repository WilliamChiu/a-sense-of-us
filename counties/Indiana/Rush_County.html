Rush County, Indiana

In 2000, 2225 people in Rush County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15757 people in Rush County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 4412 people in Putnam County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28127 people in Putnam County, Indiana lived in family households.

Rush County was formed on April 1, 1822. It was named in honor of Dr. Benjamin Rush, who signed the Declaration of Independence.
