Martin County, Indiana

In 2000, 1500 people in Martin County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8761 people in Martin County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 21456 people in Madison County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 103903 people in Madison County, Indiana lived in family households.

Martin County was formed in 1820 from Daviess and Dubois Counties. It was named for Maj. John T. Martin of Newport, Kentucky.
