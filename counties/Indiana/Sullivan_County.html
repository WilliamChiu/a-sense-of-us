Sullivan County, Indiana

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 9.815335%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2598 people in Sullivan County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 16861 people in Sullivan County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2000, 2292 people in Sullivan County, Indiana lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 21751 people in Sullivan County, Indiana lived in the county.
