Benton County, Indiana

In 2000, 1197 people in Benton County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8021 people in Benton County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 3860 people in Adams County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 30106 people in Adams County, Indiana lived in family households.

Benton County was formed February 18, 1840. It is named for Thomas H. Benton (D), U.S. Senator from Missouri.
