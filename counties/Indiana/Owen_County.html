Owen County, Indiana

In 2000, 2510 people in Owen County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18989 people in Owen County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 5791 people in Noble County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 40914 people in Noble County, Indiana lived in family households.

Owen County was formed in 1819. It was named after Abraham Owen, a colonel who died at the Battle of Tippecanoe.
