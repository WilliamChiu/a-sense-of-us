Marion County, Indiana

In 2000, 172642 people in Marion County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 669116 people in Marion County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 17299 people in LaPorte County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 87545 people in LaPorte County, Indiana lived in family households.

Marion County was created on April 1, 1822, from part of the so-called "New Purchase" lands that had been obtained by the Treaty of St. Mary's; the Lenape had previously occupied the area.
