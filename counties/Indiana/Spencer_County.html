Spencer County, Indiana

In 2000, 2115 people in Spencer County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 17913 people in Spencer County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 45486 people in St. Joseph County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 210173 people in St. Joseph County, Indiana lived in family households.

Spencer County was formed in 1818 from parts of Warrick County and Perry County. It was named for Captain Spier Spencer, killed at the Battle of Tippecanoe in 1811.
