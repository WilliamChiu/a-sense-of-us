Switzerland County, Indiana

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.9561062%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1083 people in Switzerland County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 7890 people in Switzerland County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2000, 92 people in Switzerland County, Indiana lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 9065 people in Switzerland County, Indiana lived in the county.

In 2010, 3100 people in Starke County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20247 people in Starke County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 16 people in Starke County, Indiana lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 23363 people in Starke County, Indiana lived in the county.

Switzerland County was formed in 1814. It was named for the home country of many of the early settlers.
