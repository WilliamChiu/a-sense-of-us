Clinton County, Indiana

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.21954775%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 28830 people in Clinton County, Indiana lived in family households.
