Grant County, Indiana

In 2000, 10349 people in Grant County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 58539 people in Grant County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 2624 people in Franklin County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20276 people in Franklin County, Indiana lived in family households.

Grant County was formed in 1831. It was named for Captains Samuel and Moses Grant of Kentucky.
