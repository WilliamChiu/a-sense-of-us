Henry County, Indiana

In 2000, 6358 people in Henry County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 41458 people in Henry County, Indiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 61369 people in Hancock County, Indiana lived in family households.

Henry County was formed in 1822. It was named for Patriot Patrick Henry, governor of Virginia.
