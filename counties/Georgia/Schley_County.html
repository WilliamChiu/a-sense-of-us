Schley County, Georgia

In 2000, 443 people in Schley County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3312 people in Schley County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 1302 people in Randolph County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6109 people in Randolph County, Georgia lived in family households.

The first county courthouse was built in 1858. The present Schley County Courthouse dates from 1899.
