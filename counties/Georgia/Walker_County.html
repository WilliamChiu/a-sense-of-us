Walker County, Georgia

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.6707907%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 7068 people in Walker County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 52980 people in Walker County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2000, 1005 people in Walker County, Georgia lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 61053 people in Walker County, Georgia lived in the county.

In 2010, 1301 people in Twiggs County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7633 people in Twiggs County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 89 people in Twiggs County, Georgia lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 9023 people in Twiggs County, Georgia lived in the county.
