Taliaferro County, Georgia

In 2000, 336 people in Taliaferro County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1718 people in Taliaferro County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 757 people in Stewart County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3626 people in Stewart County, Georgia lived in family households.

Taliaferro County was formed by an act of the Georgia Legislature meeting in Milledgeville on December 24, 1825. It was formed by taking portions of five other counties: Wilkes, Greene, Hancock, Oglethorpe, and Warren Counties.
