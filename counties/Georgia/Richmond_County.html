Richmond County, Georgia

In 2000, 29486 people in Richmond County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 159378 people in Richmond County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 416 people in Quitman County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2097 people in Quitman County, Georgia lived in family households.

The county is named for Charles Lennox, 3rd Duke of Richmond, a British politician and office-holder sympathetic to the cause of the American colonies. Richmond was also a first cousin to King George III.
