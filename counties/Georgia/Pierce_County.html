Pierce County, Georgia

In 2000, 1714 people in Pierce County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13807 people in Pierce County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 12567 people in Paulding County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 129095 people in Paulding County, Georgia lived in family households.

Pierce County is named for Franklin Pierce, fourteenth President of the United States. It was created December 18, 1857, from Appling and Ware counties.
