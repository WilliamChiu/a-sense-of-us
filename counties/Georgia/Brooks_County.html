Brooks County, Georgia

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -1.5010178%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 2134 people in Brooks County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13917 people in Brooks County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2000, 399 people in Brooks County, Georgia lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 16450 people in Brooks County, Georgia lived in the county.
