Muscogee County, Georgia

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -3.2962322%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 26724 people in Muscogee County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 150460 people in Muscogee County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2000, 9107 people in Muscogee County, Georgia lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 186291 people in Muscogee County, Georgia lived in the county.

In 2010, 9123 people in Montgomery County, Georgia lived in the county.
