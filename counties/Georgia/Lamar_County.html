Lamar County, Georgia

In 2000, 1676 people in Lamar County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13406 people in Lamar County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 1192 people in Jenkins County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7064 people in Jenkins County, Georgia lived in family households.
