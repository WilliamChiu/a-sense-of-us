Baldwin County, Georgia

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 20.884968%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 6427 people in Baldwin County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 30462 people in Baldwin County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2000, 7811 people in Baldwin County, Georgia lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 44700 people in Baldwin County, Georgia lived in the county.

In 2010, 1044 people in Atkinson County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7311 people in Atkinson County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 20 people in Atkinson County, Georgia lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 8375 people in Atkinson County, Georgia lived in the county.
