Laurens County, Georgia

In 2000, 5553 people in Laurens County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 38077 people in Laurens County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 3023 people in Jones County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 25325 people in Jones County, Georgia lived in family households.

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 818 sqmi, of which 807 sqmi is land and 11 sqmi (1.4%) is water. It is the third-largest county in Georgia by land area and fourth-largest by total area.
