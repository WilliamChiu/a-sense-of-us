Liberty County, Georgia

In 2000, 5629 people in Liberty County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 51197 people in Liberty County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 1224 people in Lanier County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 8594 people in Lanier County, Georgia lived in family households.

Liberty county was established in 1777. Sunbury was first designated the county seat in 1784. In 1797, the seat was transferred to Riceboro and in 1837 it was transferred again to Hinesville.
