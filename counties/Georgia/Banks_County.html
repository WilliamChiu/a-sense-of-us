Banks County, Georgia

In 2000, 1429 people in Banks County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12993 people in Banks County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 1485 people in Bacon County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9286 people in Bacon County, Georgia lived in family households.

Banks County was established in 1858. It was named for Dr. Richard E. Banks. The Georgia General Assembly passed legislation to create Banks County on February 1, 1859, from Franklin and Habersham Counties.
