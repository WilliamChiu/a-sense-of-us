Talbot County, Georgia

In 2000, 795 people in Talbot County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5686 people in Talbot County, Georgia lived in family households.

In 2010, 3582 people in Stephens County, Georgia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 22045 people in Stephens County, Georgia lived in family households.

Talbot County was created by a December 14, 1827, act of the Georgia General Assembly from a portion of Muscogee County. Taylor County was created from a portion of Talbot County in 1852.
