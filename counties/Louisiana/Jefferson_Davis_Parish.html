Jefferson Davis Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 3417 people in Jefferson Davis Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 27603 people in Jefferson Davis Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 3774 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 25634 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

Jefferson Davis Parish was one of the last incorporated parishes in the state of Louisiana. Originally a part of Imperial Calcasieu Parish, the bill creating Jefferson Datis Parish was passed in 1912 but did not take effect until 1913. Jefferson Davis Parish is also a part of the Acadiana region of Louisiana. It is named after the President of the Confederate States of America, Jefferson Davis.
