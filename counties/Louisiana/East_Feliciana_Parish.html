East Feliciana Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 1865 people in East Feliciana Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 16643 people in East Feliciana Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 3624 people in De Soto Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 22817 people in De Soto Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

The parish was part of Feliciana Parish, which was founded in 1810 and then divided, in 1824, into East Feliciana Parish and West Feliciana Parish. Feliciana is derived from a Spanish word meaning "dome".
