Bossier Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 12075 people in Bossier Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 84150 people in Bossier Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 5591 people in Avoyelles Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 33139 people in Avoyelles Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

Bossier Parish is named for Pierre Bossier, a 19th-century Louisiana state senator and U.S. representative from Natchitoches Parish.
