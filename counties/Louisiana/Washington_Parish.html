Washington Parish, Louisiana

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 2.3367643%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 5398 people in Washington Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 36741 people in Washington Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2000, 1787 people in Washington Parish, Louisiana lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 43926 people in Washington Parish, Louisiana lived in the county.

In 2010, 3118 people in Union Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 19188 people in Union Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 415 people in Union Parish, Louisiana lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 22721 people in Union Parish, Louisiana lived in the county.

Washington Parish was formed in 1819 by splitting off from St. Tammany Parish. Franklinton became the parish seat on February 10, 1821.
