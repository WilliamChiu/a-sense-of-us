Winn Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 1878 people in Winn Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13244 people in Winn Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 2947 people in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20285 people in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

Winn Parish was established in 1852 from lands which had belonged to the parishes of Catahoula, Natchitoches, and Rapides.
