Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 13387 people in Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 83926 people in Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 6358 people in St. Martin Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 45101 people in St. Martin Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

Tangipahoa Parish began in 1869, when it was carved from Livingston Parish, St. Helena Parish, St. Tammany Parish, and Washington Parish. It was flooded in 1932.
