Iberville Parish, Louisiana

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 7.27334%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 3044 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 26922 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2000, 3354 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 33320 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in the county.

In 2010, 2713 people in Franklin Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 17322 people in Franklin Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 732 people in Franklin Parish, Louisiana lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 20767 people in Franklin Parish, Louisiana lived in the county.

The parish is named for Pierre Le Moyne d'Iberville, who founded the French colony of Louisiana.
