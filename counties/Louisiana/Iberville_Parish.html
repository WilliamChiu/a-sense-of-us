Iberville Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 3044 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 26922 people in Iberville Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 2713 people in Franklin Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 17322 people in Franklin Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

The parish is named for Pierre Le Moyne d'Iberville, who founded the French colony of Louisiana.
