Caddo Parish, Louisiana

In 2000, 38729 people in Caddo Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 207035 people in Caddo Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 4264 people in Beauregard Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 30090 people in Beauregard Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 1838, Caddo Parish was carved from Natchitoches Parish; the legislature named it for the indigenous Caddo Indians.
