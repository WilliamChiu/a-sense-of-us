West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -1.721245%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 2275 people in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18756 people in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2000, 570 people in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 21601 people in West Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana lived in the county.

In 2010, 6424 people in Vernon Parish, Louisiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 43652 people in Vernon Parish, Louisiana lived in family households.

In 2010, 2258 people in Vernon Parish, Louisiana lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 52334 people in Vernon Parish, Louisiana lived in the county.
