Swift County, Minnesota

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 8.506036%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1629 people in Swift County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8789 people in Swift County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2000, 1538 people in Swift County, Minnesota lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 11956 people in Swift County, Minnesota lived in the county.

In 2010, 28261 people in Stearns County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 114168 people in Stearns County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 8213 people in Stearns County, Minnesota lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 150642 people in Stearns County, Minnesota lived in the county.

Swift County was established February 18, 1870. This county was named after Henry Adoniram Swift, the governor of Minnesota in 1863. It is located in the west central part of Minnesota and consists of 757 sqmi with 3 tiers of 7 townships each. The Swift County Courthouse was built in 1897 and was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1976. The City of Benson is the County seat of Swift County. The railroad tracks run through the center of the town of Benson and divide the downtown business districts with parks on each side of the tracks.
