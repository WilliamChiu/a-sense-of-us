Murray County, Minnesota

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.25179982%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 9165 people in Murray County, Minnesota lived in the county.
