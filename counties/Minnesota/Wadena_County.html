Wadena County, Minnesota

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 1.9174814%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2132 people in Wadena County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11136 people in Wadena County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2000, 445 people in Wadena County, Minnesota lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 13713 people in Wadena County, Minnesota lived in the county.

In 2010, 3589 people in Todd County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20960 people in Todd County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 346 people in Todd County, Minnesota lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 24895 people in Todd County, Minnesota lived in the county.

Wadena County was organized on February 21, 1873, at which time Wadena was chosen as the county seat.
