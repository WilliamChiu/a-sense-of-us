Nicollet County, Minnesota

In 2000, 4505 people in Nicollet County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

Nicollet County is a county located in the U.S. state of Minnesota. It was named for French geographer Joseph N. Nicollet. As of the 2010 census, the population was 32,727. Its county seat is St. Peter.
