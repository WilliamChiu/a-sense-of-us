Brown County, Minnesota

In 2000, 3934 people in Brown County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 21816 people in Brown County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 7154 people in Benton County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 30249 people in Benton County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Brown County was founded in 1855. It was named for Joseph Renshaw Brown, a member of the Governor's Council of Minnesota in 1855.
