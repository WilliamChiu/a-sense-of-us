Washington County, Minnesota

In 2000, 21024 people in Washington County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 176915 people in Washington County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 3327 people in Wabasha County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 18103 people in Wabasha County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Washington County was one of the nine original counties created when the Minnesota Territory was organized in 1849. The county was officially established October 27, 1849, named after George Washington.
