Roseau County, Minnesota

In 2000, 2031 people in Roseau County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14066 people in Roseau County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 2563 people in Renville County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12825 people in Renville County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Roseau County was once the home of many Chippewa, Sioux, and Mandan tribes. Archeologists have found artifacts within the county belonging to these tribes that date back 7,200 years ago.
