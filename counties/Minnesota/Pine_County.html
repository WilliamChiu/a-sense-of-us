Pine County, Minnesota

In 2000, 3700 people in Pine County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 21493 people in Pine County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 24449 people in Olmsted County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 117029 people in Olmsted County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Pine County was organized on March 1, 1856, with Chisago County being its primary parent county. Other portions of the original Pine County originated from Ramsey County. The original county seat was Chengwatana.
