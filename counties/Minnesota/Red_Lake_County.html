Red Lake County, Minnesota

In 2000, 681 people in Red Lake County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3455 people in Red Lake County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 5377 people in Polk County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 24893 people in Polk County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Red Lake County was formed on December 24, 1896 from sections of Polk County. It was named after the Red Lake River.
