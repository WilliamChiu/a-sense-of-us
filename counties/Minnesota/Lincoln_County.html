Lincoln County, Minnesota

In 2000, 941 people in Lincoln County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5283 people in Lincoln County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 2036 people in Lake County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 8606 people in Lake County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Lincoln County was created in 1873 from the western portion of Lyon County. After various changes, its county seat since 1904 has been Ivanhoe.
