Lake County, Minnesota

In 2000, 1757 people in Lake County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 9031 people in Lake County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 794 people in Kittson County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3647 people in Kittson County, Minnesota lived in family households.

Lake County was founded on February 20, 1855, as ''Superior County'', though its name was changed on March 3, of that year to ''Saint Louis County''. On March 1, 1856, Saint Louis County became Lake County, while ''Newton County'' to the west was renamed Saint Louis County.
