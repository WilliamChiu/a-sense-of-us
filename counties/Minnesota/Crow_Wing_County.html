Crow Wing County, Minnesota

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.57764053%. This change is fairly different compared to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 8630 people in Crow Wing County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 45428 people in Crow Wing County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2000, 1041 people in Crow Wing County, Minnesota lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 55099 people in Crow Wing County, Minnesota lived in the county.

In 2010, 1326 people in Clearwater County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7254 people in Clearwater County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 115 people in Clearwater County, Minnesota lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 8695 people in Clearwater County, Minnesota lived in the county.

Crow Wing county was established on May 23, 1857 and organized March 3, 1870. It is named for the Crow Wing River, which is itself named for an island in the river which is shaped like the wing of a crow.
