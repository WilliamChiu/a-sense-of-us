Goodhue County, Minnesota

In 2000, 6116 people in Goodhue County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 36911 people in Goodhue County, Minnesota lived in family households.

In 2010, 2451 people in Faribault County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11766 people in Faribault County, Minnesota lived in family households.

The county was formed from territory within Wabasha County on March 5, 1853. County boundaries were defined in 1854 and further refined again in 1855.
