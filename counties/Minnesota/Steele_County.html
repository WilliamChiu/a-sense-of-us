In 2000, 4556 people in Steele County, Minnesota lived in nonfamily households.

Steele County is a county located in the U.S. state of Minnesota. As of the 2010 census, the population was 36,576. Its county seat is Owatonna. The county was named for Franklin Steele, a prominent early resident of the state.
