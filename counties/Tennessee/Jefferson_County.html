Jefferson County, Tennessee

In 2000, 5447 people in Jefferson County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 37185 people in Jefferson County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 1228 people in Houston County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7021 people in Houston County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Jefferson County was established on June 11, 1792, by William Blount, Governor of the Southwest Territory. It had been a part of Caswell County during the State of Franklin period (1784–1789). Its county seat, Dandridge, was settled in 1783.
