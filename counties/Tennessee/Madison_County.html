Madison County, Tennessee

In 2000, 12978 people in Madison County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 75557 people in Madison County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 7209 people in McMinn County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 44115 people in McMinn County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Madison County was formed in 1821, and named for founding father and president, James Madison. The county was part of lands purchased from the Chickasaw in 1818.
