Lincoln County, Tennessee

In 2000, 3864 people in Lincoln County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 27008 people in Lincoln County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 3426 people in Lauderdale County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 21655 people in Lauderdale County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Lincoln County was created in 1809 from parts of Bedford County. The land occupied by the county was part of a land session obtained from the Cherokee and Chickasaw in 1806.
