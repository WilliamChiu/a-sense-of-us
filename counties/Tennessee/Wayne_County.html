Wayne County, Tennessee

In 2000, 1814 people in Wayne County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12861 people in Wayne County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 723 people in Van Buren County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4731 people in Van Buren County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Wayne County was created in 1817 from parts of Hickman and Humphreys counties. Waynesboro, its county seat, was established in 1821. The city of Clifton emerged as a key river port in the mid-19th century.
