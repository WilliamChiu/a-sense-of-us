Cannon County, Tennessee

In 2000, 1550 people in Cannon County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11111 people in Cannon County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 17289 people in Blount County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 103694 people in Blount County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Cannon County was established by the Tennessee state legislature on January 31, 1836. It was formed from portions of Rutherford, Smith, and Warren Counties and was named for Governor Newton Cannon.
