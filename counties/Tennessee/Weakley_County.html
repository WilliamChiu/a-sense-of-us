Weakley County, Tennessee

In 2000, 5527 people in Weakley County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 26869 people in Weakley County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 5898 people in Warren County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 33361 people in Warren County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Weakley County was established in 1823. The county was the state's largest corn producer during the 19th century, though soybeans became the county's leading crop in the latter half of the 20th century.
