Lawrence County, Tennessee

In 2000, 4675 people in Lawrence County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 34884 people in Lawrence County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 85280 people in Knox County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 334598 people in Knox County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Created by an act of the Tennessee General Assembly on October 21, 1817, Lawrence County was formed from lands previously part of Hickman and Giles counties.
