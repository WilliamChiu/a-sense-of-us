Unicoi County, Tennessee

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.8881211%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 14815 people in Unicoi County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2000, 17667 people in Unicoi County, Tennessee lived in the county.

In 2010, 160645 people in Sumner County, Tennessee lived in the county.

Unicoi County was created in 1875 from portions of Washington and Carter counties, though the first settlers had arrived more than century earlier. The county remained predominantly agrarian until the arrival of railroads in the area in the 1880s.
