Coffee County, Tennessee

In 2000, 6250 people in Coffee County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 40980 people in Coffee County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 4488 people in Claiborne County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 26468 people in Claiborne County, Tennessee lived in family households.

Coffee County was formed in 1836 from parts of Bedford, Warren, and Franklin counties. It was named for John Coffee.
