McNairy County, Tennessee

In 2000, 3189 people in McNairy County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 20936 people in McNairy County, Tennessee lived in family households.

In 2010, 4553 people in Lincoln County, Tennessee lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28525 people in Lincoln County, Tennessee lived in family households.

McNairy County was formed in 1823 from parts of Hardin County, and was named for Judge John McNairy.
