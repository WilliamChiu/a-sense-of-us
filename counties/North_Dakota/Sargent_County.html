Sargent County, North Dakota

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -1.5321851%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 4366 people in Sargent County, North Dakota lived in the county.
