Sargent County, North Dakota

In 2000, 596 people in Sargent County, North Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3741 people in Sargent County, North Dakota lived in family households.

In 2010, 437 people in Renville County, North Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1979 people in Renville County, North Dakota lived in family households.

In 1997 the Brampton Lutheran Church (originally built in 1908) was moved to Sletta near Radøy in Norway and reconstructed there. It is now called ''Emigrantkirka'' (Emigrant Church).
