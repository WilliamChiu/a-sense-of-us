Stark County, North Dakota

In 2000, 3651 people in Stark County, North Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18114 people in Stark County, North Dakota lived in family households.

In 2010, 245 people in Sheridan County, North Dakota lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1076 people in Sheridan County, North Dakota lived in family households.

Stark County was created February 10, 1879, as a county within Dakota Territory from parts of Howard County and Williams County.
