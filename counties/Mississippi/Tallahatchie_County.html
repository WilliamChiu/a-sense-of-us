Tallahatchie County, Mississippi

In 2000, 1597 people in Tallahatchie County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13203 people in Tallahatchie County, Mississippi lived in family households.

In 2010, 1799 people in Smith County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14585 people in Smith County, Mississippi lived in family households.

The county was founded on December 31, 1833. Tallahatchie is a Choctaw name meaning "rock of waters".ttp://www.hal-pc.org/~asbalch/historytallahatchieco-1.html The county is one of ten in Mississippi with two county seats, Charleston and Sumner. Charleston was the first county seat, and Sumner was organized later in 1872.
