Hinds County, Mississippi

In 2000, 34460 people in Hinds County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

Hinds County is a county located in the State of Mississippi. As of the 2010 census, the population was 245,285, making it the most populous county in Mississippi. Its county seats are Raymond and Jackson, the state capital. The county is named for General Thomas Hinds, a hero of the Battle of New Orleans during the War of 1812.
