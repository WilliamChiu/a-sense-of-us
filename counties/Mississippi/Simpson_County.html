Simpson County, Mississippi

In 2000, 3043 people in Simpson County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

Simpson County is a county located in the U.S. state of Mississippi. As of the 2010 census, the population was 27,503. The county seat is Mendenhall. The county is named for a judge, Josiah Simpson.
