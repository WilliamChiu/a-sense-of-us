Smith County, Mississippi

In 2000, 1616 people in Smith County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14435 people in Smith County, Mississippi lived in family households.

In 2010, 3746 people in Scott County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 24289 people in Scott County, Mississippi lived in family households.

Smith County is named for Major David Smith. The county is well known among Mississippians for its Smith County Watermelons.
