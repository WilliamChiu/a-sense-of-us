Chickasaw County, Mississippi

In 2000, 2163 people in Chickasaw County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 17069 people in Chickasaw County, Mississippi lived in family households.

In 2010, 5074 people in Bolivar County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 27507 people in Bolivar County, Mississippi lived in family households.

The County was created in 1836 by Mississippi Legislature and was quickly settled by Americans from the East Coast. By the time of the Civil War there were many large cotton plantations and the slave population had grown larger than the free population.
