Issaquena County, Mississippi

In 2000, 248 people in Issaquena County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1763 people in Issaquena County, Mississippi lived in family households.

In 2010, 37264 people in Hinds County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 200178 people in Hinds County, Mississippi lived in family households.

"Issaquena" is a Choctaw word meaning "Deer River". The Choctaw people were the first inhabitants of the county, and were removed from their land in 1820. Non-Native settlers began arriving in the early 1830s.
