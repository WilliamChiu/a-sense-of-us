Jefferson County, Mississippi

Jefferson County is a county located in the U.S. state of Mississippi. As of the 2010 census, the population was 7,726, making it the third-least populous county in Mississippi. Its county seat is Fayette. The county is named for U.S. President Thomas Jefferson.
