Rankin County, Mississippi

In 2000, 13132 people in Rankin County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 97326 people in Rankin County, Mississippi lived in family households.

In 2010, 3548 people in Pontotoc County, Mississippi lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 26173 people in Pontotoc County, Mississippi lived in family households.

Rankin County is a county located in the state of Mississippi. As of the 2010 census, the population was 141,617, making it the fourth-most populous county in Mississippi. The county seat is Brandon. The county is named in honor of Christopher Rankin, a Mississippi Congressman who served from 1819 to 1826.
