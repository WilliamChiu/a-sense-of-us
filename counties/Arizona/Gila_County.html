Gila County, Arizona

In 2000, 7102 people in Gila County, Arizona lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 43302 people in Gila County, Arizona lived in family households.

In 2010, 20672 people in Cochise County, Arizona lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 104403 people in Cochise County, Arizona lived in family households.

The county was formed from parts of Maricopa County and Pinal County on February 8, 1881. The boundary was then extended eastward to the San Carlos River by public petition in 1889. The original county seat was in the mining community of Globe City, now Globe, Arizona.
