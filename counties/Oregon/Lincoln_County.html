Lincoln County, Oregon

In 2000, 8807 people in Lincoln County, Oregon lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 35030 people in Lincoln County, Oregon lived in family households.

In 2010, 12028 people in Klamath County, Oregon lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 53402 people in Klamath County, Oregon lived in family households.

Lincoln County was created by the Oregon Legislative Assembly on February 20, 1893, from the western portion of Benton and Polk counties. The county adjusted its boundaries in 1923, 1925, 1927, 1931, and 1949.
