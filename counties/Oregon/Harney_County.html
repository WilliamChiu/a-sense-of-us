Harney County, Oregon

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.6490946%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1147 people in Harney County, Oregon lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6293 people in Harney County, Oregon lived in family households.

In 2000, 169 people in Harney County, Oregon lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 7609 people in Harney County, Oregon lived in the county.

In 2010, 18468 people in Douglas County, Oregon lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1708 people in Douglas County, Oregon lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 107667 people in Douglas County, Oregon lived in the county.
