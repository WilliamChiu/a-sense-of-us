Coos County, Oregon

In 2000, 10891 people in Coos County, Oregon lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 50458 people in Coos County, Oregon lived in family households.

In 2010, 315893 people in Clackamas County, Oregon lived in family households.

It's unclear where the name Coos originated. Lewis and Clark noted Cook-koo-oose. Early maps and documents spelled it Kowes, Cowes, Coose, Koos, among others.
