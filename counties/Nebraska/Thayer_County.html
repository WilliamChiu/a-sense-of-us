Thayer County is a county located in the U.S. state of Nebraska. As of the 2010 census, the population was 5,228. Its county seat is Hebron. The county was created in 1856 and originally named Jefferson County. It was later organized in 1870-1871 and renamed for the General and Governor John Milton Thayer.
