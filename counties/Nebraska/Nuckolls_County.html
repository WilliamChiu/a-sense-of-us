Nuckolls County, Nebraska

In 2000, 839 people in Nuckolls County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 4174 people in Nuckolls County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 753 people in Morrill County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4207 people in Morrill County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Nuckolls County was formed in 1860 and later organized in 1871. It was named after Stephen F. Nuckolls, an early pioneer in this area.
