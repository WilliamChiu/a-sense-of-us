Keith County, Nebraska

In 2000, 1331 people in Keith County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 7444 people in Keith County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 1416 people in Jefferson County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6026 people in Jefferson County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Keith County was formed in 1873. Sources differ on the Keith after whom it was named: either M. C. Keith of North Platte, whose grandson Keith Neville became Nebraska's 18th governor in 1917; or John Keith, also of North Platte.
