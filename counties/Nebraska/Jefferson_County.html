Jefferson County, Nebraska

In 2000, 1349 people in Jefferson County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6818 people in Jefferson County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 1711 people in Holt County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 8564 people in Holt County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Jefferson County was founded on January 26, 1856 and later organized in 1864. It was named for Thomas Jefferson, third president of the United States.
