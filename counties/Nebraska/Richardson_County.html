Richardson County, Nebraska

In 2000, 1611 people in Richardson County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 7716 people in Richardson County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 4624 people in Platte County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 27137 people in Platte County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Richardson County was formed in 1855. It was named after William A. Richardson, a governor of the Nebraska Territory.
