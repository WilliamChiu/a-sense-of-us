Dundy County, Nebraska

In 2000, 358 people in Dundy County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1847 people in Dundy County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 831 people in Dixon County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 5088 people in Dixon County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Dundy County was formed in 1873 and attached to neighboring Hitchcock County. It was named after Judge Elmer S. Dundy. The county was later organized in 1884.
