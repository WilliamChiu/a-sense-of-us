Sarpy County, Nebraska

In 2000, 12922 people in Sarpy County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 108372 people in Sarpy County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 1627 people in Richardson County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6581 people in Richardson County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Sarpy County is named after Colonel Peter Sarpy, an early fur trader at Fontenelle's Post located in the Bellevue area in the 1840s. Sarpy died in Plattsmouth, Nebraska Territory in 1865.
