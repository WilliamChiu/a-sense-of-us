Morrill County, Nebraska

In 2000, 728 people in Morrill County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 4604 people in Morrill County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 55 people in McPherson County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 484 people in McPherson County, Nebraska lived in family households.

The Battle of Mud Springs and the Battle of Rush Creek between the U.S. army and the Cheyenne, Lakota Sioux, and Arapaho Indians took place in 1865 within what would become Morrill county.
