Brown County, Nebraska

In 2000, 594 people in Brown County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4534 people in Boone County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Brown County is a county located in the U.S. state of Nebraska. As of the 2010 census, the population was 3,145. Its county seat is Ainsworth. The county was established in 1883 and named for two members of the legislature who reported the bill for organization of the county.
