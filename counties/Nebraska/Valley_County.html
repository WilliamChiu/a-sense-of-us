Valley County, Nebraska

In 2000, 732 people in Valley County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3830 people in Valley County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 914 people in Thayer County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4158 people in Thayer County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Valley County was formed in 1871 and later organized in 1873. Its name derives from the local terrain: much of the county consists of valley land lying between the North and Middle Loup Rivers.
