Garden County, Nebraska

In 2000, 393 people in Garden County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1839 people in Garden County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 499 people in Frontier County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 2144 people in Frontier County, Nebraska lived in family households.

Garden County was formed in 1909 by popular vote. Voters in the general election of November 2, 1909, approved making the northern part of Deuel County into its own county. It is said that the county was so named in the hope that this land should become the garden of the West or with allusion to the "Garden of Eden".
