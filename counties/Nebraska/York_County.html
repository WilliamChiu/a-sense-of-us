York County, Nebraska

In 2000, 2078 people in York County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11795 people in York County, Nebraska lived in family households.

In 2010, 1947 people in Wayne County, Nebraska lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6386 people in Wayne County, Nebraska lived in family households.

York County was organized in 1870. Sources differ on the origin of the county's name: some state that it was named after York in England, while others maintain that it was named by early settlers from York County, Pennsylvania.
