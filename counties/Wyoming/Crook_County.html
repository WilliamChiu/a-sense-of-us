Crook County, Wyoming

In 2000, 760 people in Crook County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5027 people in Crook County, Wyoming lived in family households.

In 2010, 2753 people in Carbon County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12352 people in Carbon County, Wyoming lived in family households.

Crook County was created by on the legislature of the Wyoming Territory on December 8, 1868, from portions of Albany and Laramie Counties.
