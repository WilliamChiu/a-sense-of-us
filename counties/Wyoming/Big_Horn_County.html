Big Horn County, Wyoming

In 2000, 1407 people in Big Horn County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 9807 people in Big Horn County, Wyoming lived in family households.

In 2010, 13077 people in Wood County, Wisconsin lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 60781 people in Wood County, Wisconsin lived in family households.

Big Horn County was created by the legislature of Wyoming Territory on March 12, 1890. The county was created with lands ceded by Fremont County, Johnson County, and Sheridan County.
