Hot Springs County, Wyoming

In 2000, 858 people in Hot Springs County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 3879 people in Hot Springs County, Wyoming lived in family households.

In 2010, 6272 people in Fremont County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 32987 people in Fremont County, Wyoming lived in family households.

Hot Springs County was created on February 21, 1911 with land detached from Big Horn County, Fremont County, and Park County.
