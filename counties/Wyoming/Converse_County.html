Converse County, Wyoming

In 2000, 1516 people in Converse County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10442 people in Converse County, Wyoming lived in family households.

In 2010, 7133 people in Campbell County, Wyoming lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 38578 people in Campbell County, Wyoming lived in family households.

Converse County was created on March 9, 1888 by the legislature of the Wyoming Territory. The county was formed from land ceded by Albany County and Laramie County
