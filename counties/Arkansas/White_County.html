White County, Arkansas

In 2000, 7908 people in White County, Arkansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 55700 people in White County, Arkansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 2920 people in Van Buren County, Arkansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14186 people in Van Buren County, Arkansas lived in family households.

On May 17, 1862, White County was the site of the Little Red Skirmish between Union Major General Samule J Curtis and a force of about 100 loosely organized rebels, followed by the Action at Whitney Lane in June., also known as The Skirmish at Searcy Landing.
