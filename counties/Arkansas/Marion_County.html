Marion County, Arkansas

In 2000, 2188 people in Marion County, Arkansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13799 people in Marion County, Arkansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 7663 people in Lonoke County, Arkansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 60121 people in Lonoke County, Arkansas lived in family households.

Marion County was formerly home to a large zinc mining industry. The ruins of the Rush Creek mining district are preserved as the Rush Historic District, which is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.
