Sebastian County, Arkansas

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 1.3434649%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 17249 people in Sebastian County, Arkansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 95498 people in Sebastian County, Arkansas lived in family households.

In 2000, 2324 people in Sebastian County, Arkansas lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 115071 people in Sebastian County, Arkansas lived in the county.

In 2010, 1577 people in Scott County, Arkansas lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9577 people in Scott County, Arkansas lived in family households.

In 2010, 79 people in Scott County, Arkansas lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 11233 people in Scott County, Arkansas lived in the county.

Sebastian County is Arkansas's 56th county, formed on January 6, 1851, and named for William K. Sebastian, United States Senator from Arkansas.
