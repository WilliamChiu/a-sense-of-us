Elko County, Nevada

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.286901%. This change is reasionably similar to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 5236 people in Elko County, Nevada lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 39261 people in Elko County, Nevada lived in family households.

In 2000, 794 people in Elko County, Nevada lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 45291 people in Elko County, Nevada lived in the county.
