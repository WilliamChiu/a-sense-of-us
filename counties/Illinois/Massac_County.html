Massac County, Illinois

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.2872944%. This change is reasionably similar to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2173 people in Massac County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12654 people in Massac County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2000, 334 people in Massac County, Illinois lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 15161 people in Massac County, Illinois lived in the county.

In 2010, 6344 people in Marion County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 32335 people in Marion County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 758 people in Marion County, Illinois lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 39437 people in Marion County, Illinois lived in the county.
