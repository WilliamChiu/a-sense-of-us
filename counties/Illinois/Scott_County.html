Scott County, Illinois

In 2000, 746 people in Scott County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 4735 people in Scott County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 4301 people in Saline County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 19734 people in Saline County, Illinois lived in family households.

Scott County was formed in 1839 out of Morgan County. It was named for a local pioneer family named Scott.
