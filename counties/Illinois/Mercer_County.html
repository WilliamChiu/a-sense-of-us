Mercer County, Illinois

In 2000, 1963 people in Mercer County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14781 people in Mercer County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 2409 people in Mason County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12051 people in Mason County, Illinois lived in family households.

In May 1812, Congress passed an act which set aside lands in Arkansas, Michigan, and Illinois as payment to volunteer soldiers in the War of 1812. Mercer County was part of this "Military Tract."
