Mason County, Illinois

In 2000, 2122 people in Mason County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13697 people in Mason County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 45778 people in Madison County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 219750 people in Madison County, Illinois lived in family households.

Mason County was created in 1841 out of portions of Tazewell and Menard counties.
