Richland County, Illinois

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.5935073%. This change is fairly different compared to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2488 people in Richland County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13511 people in Richland County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2000, 150 people in Richland County, Illinois lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 16149 people in Richland County, Illinois lived in the county.

In 2010, 1110 people in Pulaski County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 5030 people in Pulaski County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 21 people in Pulaski County, Illinois lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 6161 people in Pulaski County, Illinois lived in the county.

Richland County was established on February 24, 1824, out of portions of Clay and Lawrence counties.
