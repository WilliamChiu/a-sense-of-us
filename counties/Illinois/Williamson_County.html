Williamson County, Illinois

In 2000, 9678 people in Williamson County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 49965 people in Williamson County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 2461 people in White County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11813 people in White County, Illinois lived in family households.

Williamson County was formed out of Franklin County on February 28, 1839, and was named for Williamson County, Tennessee.
