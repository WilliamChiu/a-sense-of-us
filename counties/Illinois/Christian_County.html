Christian County, Illinois

In 2000, 5083 people in Christian County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 28479 people in Christian County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 2670 people in Carroll County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12494 people in Carroll County, Illinois lived in family households.

Christian County was formed February 15, 1839 out of Sangamon, Montgomery and Shelby Counties. It was named for Christian County, Kentucky.
