Hamilton County, Illinois

In 2000, 1120 people in Hamilton County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 7296 people in Hamilton County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 967 people in Gallatin County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4597 people in Gallatin County, Illinois lived in family households.

Hamilton County was formed out of White County in 1821. It is named for Alexander Hamilton, Revolutionary War hero and the first United States Secretary of the Treasury.
