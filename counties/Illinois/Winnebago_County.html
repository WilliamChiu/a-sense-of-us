Winnebago County, Illinois

In 2000, 41732 people in Winnebago County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 231612 people in Winnebago County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 9197 people in Whiteside County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 48289 people in Whiteside County, Illinois lived in family households.

Winnebago County was formed on January 16, 1836 out of Jo Daviess and LaSalle counties. It was named for the Winnebago Tribe of American Indians.
