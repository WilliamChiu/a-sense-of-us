Grundy County, Illinois

In 2000, 4861 people in Grundy County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 32327 people in Grundy County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 5674 people in Fulton County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28712 people in Fulton County, Illinois lived in family households.

Grundy County was established on February 17, 1841. It was formed out of LaSalle County and named after U.S. Attorney General Felix Grundy (1777-1840).
