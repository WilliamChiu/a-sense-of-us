De Witt County, Illinois

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -6.05641%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 2437 people in De Witt County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14095 people in De Witt County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2000, 266 people in De Witt County, Illinois lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 16798 people in De Witt County, Illinois lived in the county.

In 2010, 3076 people in Crawford County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 15246 people in Crawford County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 1495 people in Crawford County, Illinois lived in group quarters.
