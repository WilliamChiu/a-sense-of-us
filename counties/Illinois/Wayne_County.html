Wayne County, Illinois

In 2000, 2417 people in Wayne County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14527 people in Wayne County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 1994 people in Wabash County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9865 people in Wabash County, Illinois lived in family households.

Wayne County was formed in 1819 out of Edwards County. It is named after Gen. "Mad Anthony" Wayne, an officer in the Revolutionary War and Northwest Indian War.
