Franklin County, Illinois

In 2000, 6121 people in Franklin County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 32332 people in Franklin County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 4961 people in Effingham County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28841 people in Effingham County, Illinois lived in family households.

Franklin County was established on January 2, 1818 and formed from parts of Gallatin and White Counties. It was named for Benjamin Franklin.
