McLean County, Illinois

In 2000, 29716 people in McLean County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 109424 people in McLean County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 4507 people in Logan County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 21437 people in Logan County, Illinois lived in family households.

McLean County was formed late in 1830 out of Tazewell County. It was named for John McLean, United States Senator for Illinois, who died in 1830.
