Warren County, Illinois

In 2000, 2573 people in Warren County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14939 people in Warren County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 2776 people in Union County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14466 people in Union County, Illinois lived in family households.

Warren County was organized in 1825 out of Pike County. Pike County, consisted of all portions of state North and West of the Illinois River before 1825. Henderson County was formed in 1841 from the western area of Warren County.
