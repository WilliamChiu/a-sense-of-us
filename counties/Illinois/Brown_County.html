Brown County, Illinois

In 2000, 835 people in Brown County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 4143 people in Brown County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 1375 people in Alexander County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6320 people in Alexander County, Illinois lived in family households.

Brown County was formed out of Schuyler County in 1839. It is named in honor of U.S. General Jacob Brown, who defeated the British at the Battle of Sackett's Harbor in 1813.
