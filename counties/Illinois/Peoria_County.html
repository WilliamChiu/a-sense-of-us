Peoria County, Illinois

In 2000, 31007 people in Peoria County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 145546 people in Peoria County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 6262 people in Morgan County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 26221 people in Morgan County, Illinois lived in family households.

Peoria County was formed in 1825 out of Fulton County. It was named for the Peoria, an Illiniwek people who lived there. It included most of the western valley of the Illinois River up to the Chicago river portage.
