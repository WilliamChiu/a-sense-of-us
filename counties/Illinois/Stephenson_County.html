Stephenson County, Illinois

In 2000, 7379 people in Stephenson County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 40780 people in Stephenson County, Illinois lived in family households.

In 2010, 817 people in Scott County, Illinois lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4495 people in Scott County, Illinois lived in family households.

Stephenson County was formed in 1837 out of Jo Daviess and Winnebago counties. The county was named for Colonel Benjamin Stephenson, an official of the Illinois Territory.
