Tuscola County, Michigan

In 2000, 6506 people in Tuscola County, Michigan lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 50299 people in Tuscola County, Michigan lived in family households.

In 2010, 6181 people in Sanilac County, Michigan lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 36367 people in Sanilac County, Michigan lived in family households.

The name Tuscola was a Neologism created by Henry Schoolcraft. One scholar believes it to be a combination of "dusinagon" (level) and "cola" (lands).
