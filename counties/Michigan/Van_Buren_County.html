Van Buren County, Michigan

In 2000, 9397 people in Van Buren County, Michigan lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 64950 people in Van Buren County, Michigan lived in family households.

In 2010, 1546 people in Schoolcraft County, Michigan lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6799 people in Schoolcraft County, Michigan lived in family households.

The county was named for Martin Van Buren before he became president. He was U.S. Secretary of State and later Vice President under President Andrew Jackson, and thus Van Buren is one of Michigan's "Cabinet counties".
