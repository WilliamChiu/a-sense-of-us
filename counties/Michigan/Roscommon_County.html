Roscommon County, Michigan

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.1344502%. This change is fairly different compared to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 4204 people in Roscommon County, Michigan lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 20932 people in Roscommon County, Michigan lived in family households.

In 2000, 333 people in Roscommon County, Michigan lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 25469 people in Roscommon County, Michigan lived in the county.
