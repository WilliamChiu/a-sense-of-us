Iosco County is a county located in the U.S. state of Michigan. As of the 2010 census, the population was 25,887. The county seat is Tawas City. In 1840 it was set off as Kanotin County, and renamed Iosco in 1843. The county was organized in 1857. ''Iosco'' is a Native American word meaning "water of light".
