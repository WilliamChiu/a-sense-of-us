Leelanau County, Michigan

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.58398247%. This change is fairly different compared to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2636 people in Leelanau County, Michigan lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 18263 people in Leelanau County, Michigan lived in family households.

In 2000, 220 people in Leelanau County, Michigan lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 21119 people in Leelanau County, Michigan lived in the county.
