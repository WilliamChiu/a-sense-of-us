Sutter County, California

In 2000, 8901 people in Sutter County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 68646 people in Sutter County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 92978 people in Sonoma County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 380857 people in Sonoma County, California lived in family households.

Sutter County was one of the original counties of California, created in 1850 at the time of statehood. Parts of the county were given to Placer County in 1852.
