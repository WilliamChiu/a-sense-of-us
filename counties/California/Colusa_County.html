Colusa County, California

In 2000, 1837 people in Colusa County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 16520 people in Colusa County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 51453 people in Butte County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 163605 people in Butte County, California lived in family households.

Colusa County is one of the original counties of California, created in 1850 at the time of statehood. Parts of the county's territory were given to Tehama County in 1856 and to Glenn County in 1891.
