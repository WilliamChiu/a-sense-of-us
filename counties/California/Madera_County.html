Madera County, California

In 2000, 9983 people in Madera County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 105026 people in Madera County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 4116 people in Lassen County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 21000 people in Lassen County, California lived in family households.

Madera County was formed in 1893 from the southern part of Mariposa County during a special election held on May 16, 1893. Citizens residing in the area that was to become Madera County voted 1,179 to 358 for establishment of the new county.
