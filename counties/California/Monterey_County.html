Monterey County, California

In 2000, 43822 people in Monterey County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 336964 people in Monterey County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 1763 people in Modoc County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7566 people in Modoc County, California lived in family households.

Monterey County was one of the original counties of California, created in 1850 at the time of statehood. Parts of the county were given to San Benito County in 1874.
