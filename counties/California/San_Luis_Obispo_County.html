San Luis Obispo County, California

In 2000, 49371 people in San Luis Obispo County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 181739 people in San Luis Obispo County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 283293 people in San Francisco County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 497678 people in San Francisco County, California lived in family households.

The prehistory of San Luis Obispo County is strongly influenced by the Chumash people who had significant settlement here at least as early as the Millingstone Horizon thousands of years before the present age. Important settlements existed, for example, in many coastal areas such as Morro Bay and Los Osos.
