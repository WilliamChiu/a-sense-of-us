Siskiyou County, California

In 2000, 7557 people in Siskiyou County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 36054 people in Siskiyou County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 30550 people in Shasta County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 144019 people in Shasta County, California lived in family households.

Siskiyou County was created on March 22, 1852, from parts of Shasta and Klamath Counties, and named after the Siskiyou mountain range. Parts of the county's territory were given to Modoc County in 1855.
