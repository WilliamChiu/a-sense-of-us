San Benito County, California

In 2000, 4119 people in San Benito County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 48608 people in San Benito County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 237105 people in Riverside County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1916707 people in Riverside County, California lived in family households.

The county is named after the San Benito Valley. Father Juan Crespí, in his expedition in 1772, named a small river in honor of San Benedicto (Saint Benedict), the patron saint of the married, and it is from the contraction of this name that the county took its name.
