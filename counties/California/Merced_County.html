Merced County, California

In 2000, 18006 people in Merced County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 189693 people in Merced County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 3441 people in Mariposa County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14085 people in Mariposa County, California lived in family households.

Merced County was formed in 1855 from parts of Mariposa County. Parts of its territory were given to Fresno County in 1856.
