San Francisco County, California

In 2000, 264715 people in San Francisco County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 492261 people in San Francisco County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 188464 people in San Bernardino County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1806692 people in San Bernardino County, California lived in family households.

The earliest archaeological evidence of human habitation of the territory of the city of San Francisco dates to 3000 BC. The Yelamu group of the Ohlone people resided in a few small villages when an overland Spanish exploration party, led by Don Gaspar de Portolà arrived on November 2, 1769, the first documented European visit to San Francisco Bay.
