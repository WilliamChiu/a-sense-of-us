Santa Clara County, California

In 2000, 241958 people in Santa Clara County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 1410913 people in Santa Clara County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 108902 people in San Mateo County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 600696 people in San Mateo County, California lived in family households.

Santa Clara County was one of the original counties of California, formed in 1850 at the time of statehood. The original inhabitants included the Ohlone, residing on Coyote Creek and Calaveras Creek. Part of the county's territory was given to Alameda County in 1853.
