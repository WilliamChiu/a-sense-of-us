Household Populations

Yuba County, California

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.94879866%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 7524 people in Yuba County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 51361 people in Yuba County, California lived in family households.

In 2000, 1334 people in Yuba County, California lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 60219 people in Yuba County, California lived in the county.

In 2010, 94089 people in Ventura County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 718629 people in Ventura County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 10600 people in Ventura County, California lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 823318 people in Ventura County, California lived in the county.

Yuba County was one of the original counties of California, formed in 1850 at the time of statehood. Parts of the county's territory were given to Placer County in 1851, to Nevada County in 1851 and to Sierra County in 1852.
