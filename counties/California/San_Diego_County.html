San Diego County, California

In 2000, 453538 people in San Diego County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2263282 people in San Diego County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 4429 people in San Benito County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 50551 people in San Benito County, California lived in family households.

The area which is now San Diego County has been inhabited for more than 10,000 years by Kumeyaay (also called Diegueño), Luiseño, Cupeño and Cahuilla Indians.
