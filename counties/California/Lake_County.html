Lake County, California

In 2000, 10598 people in Lake County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 46622 people in Lake County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 81834 people in Kern County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 721040 people in Kern County, California lived in family households.

Lake County was formed in 1861 from parts of Napa and Mendocino counties, but the area had European-American settlers from at least the 1840s. Lake County has long been known as a farming community.
