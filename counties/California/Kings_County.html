Kings County, California

In 2000, 9837 people in Kings County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 99495 people in Kings County, California lived in family households.

In 2010, 3785 people in Inyo County, California lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14328 people in Inyo County, California lived in family households.

The area was inhabited for thousands of years by American Indians including the Tachi Yokuts tribe. It was colonized by Spain, Mexico and the United States.
