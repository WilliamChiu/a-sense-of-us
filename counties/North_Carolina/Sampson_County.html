Sampson County, North Carolina

In 2000, 7253 people in Sampson County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 51559 people in Sampson County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 14636 people in Rockingham County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 77952 people in Rockingham County, North Carolina lived in family households.

Sampson County was formally and legally established in April of 1784 by the North Carolina General Assembly from an area taken from neighboring Duplin County. Land from neighboring Wayne County and New Hanover counties would be annexed later.
