Edgecombe County, North Carolina

In 2000, 6449 people in Edgecombe County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 48035 people in Edgecombe County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 5068 people in Davie County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 35807 people in Davie County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1741 from Bertie County. It was named for Richard Edgcumbe, a Member of Parliament from 1701 to 1742 and a lord of the treasury, who became 1st Baron Edgcumbe in 1742.
