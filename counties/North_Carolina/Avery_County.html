Avery County, North Carolina

In 2000, 2306 people in Avery County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12987 people in Avery County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 1757 people in Alleghany County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9287 people in Alleghany County, North Carolina lived in family households.
