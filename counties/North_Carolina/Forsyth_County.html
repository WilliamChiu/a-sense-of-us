Forsyth County, North Carolina

In 2000, 50421 people in Forsyth County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 245697 people in Forsyth County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 8308 people in Duplin County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 49518 people in Duplin County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1849 from Stokes County. It was named for Major Benjamin Forsyth, who was killed in the War of 1812.
