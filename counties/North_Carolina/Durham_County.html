Durham County, North Carolina

In 2000, 46317 people in Durham County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 167187 people in Durham County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 22122 people in Davidson County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 139065 people in Davidson County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed on April 17, 1881, from parts of Orange County and Wake County, taking the name of its own county seat. In 1911 parts of Cedar Fork Township of Wake County was transferred to Durham County and became Carr Township.
