Chatham County, North Carolina

In 2000, 7302 people in Chatham County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 41424 people in Chatham County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 11878 people in Carteret County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 53604 people in Carteret County, North Carolina lived in family households.

Some of the first European settlers of what would become the county were English Quakers, who settled along the Haw and Eno rivers.
