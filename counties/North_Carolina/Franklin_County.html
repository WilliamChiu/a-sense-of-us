Franklin County, North Carolina

In 2000, 5997 people in Franklin County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 40012 people in Franklin County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 59358 people in Durham County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 198108 people in Durham County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1779 from the southern half of Bute County. It is named for Benjamin Franklin. It is a part of the Research Triangle.
