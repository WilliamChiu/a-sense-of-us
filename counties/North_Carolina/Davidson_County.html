Davidson County, North Carolina

In 2000, 18579 people in Davidson County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 126771 people in Davidson County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 47739 people in Cumberland County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 261535 people in Cumberland County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The original North Carolina county of this name was created in 1786 what was then the far western portion of North Carolina, with its county seat at Nashville and a territory covering most of what is now Middle Tennessee. When Tennessee was established as a separate state in 1796, this county became Davidson County, Tennessee.
