Lincoln County, North Carolina

In 2000, 7217 people in Lincoln County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 55672 people in Lincoln County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 1556 people in Jones County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 8506 people in Jones County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1779 from the eastern part of Tryon County. It was named for Benjamin Lincoln, a general in the American Revolutionary War.
