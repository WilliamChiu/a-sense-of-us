Cabarrus County, North Carolina

In 2000, 15834 people in Cabarrus County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 113060 people in Cabarrus County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 17130 people in Brunswick County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 89458 people in Brunswick County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed on December 29, 1792 from Mecklenburg County. It was named after Stephen Cabarrus of Chowan County, speaker of the North Carolina House of Commons.
