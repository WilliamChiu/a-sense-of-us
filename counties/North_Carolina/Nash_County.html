Nash County, North Carolina

In 2000, 11389 people in Nash County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 73904 people in Nash County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 2421 people in Mitchell County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12954 people in Mitchell County, North Carolina lived in family households.

Nash County was formed in 1777 from Edgecombe County. It was named for American Revolutionary War Brigadier General Francis Nash, who was mortally wounded at the Battle of Germantown that year.
