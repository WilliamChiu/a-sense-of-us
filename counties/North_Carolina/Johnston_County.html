Johnston County, North Carolina

In 2000, 15915 people in Johnston County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 104333 people in Johnston County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 921 people in Hyde County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4059 people in Hyde County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1746 from Craven County. It was named for Gabriel Johnston, Governor of North Carolina from 1734 to 1752.
