Alleghany County, North Carolina

In 2000, 1628 people in Alleghany County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8840 people in Alleghany County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 3787 people in Yates County, New York lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20274 people in Yates County, New York lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1859 from the eastern part of Ashe County, North Carolina. It was named for the Allegheny Mountains. Numerous boundary adjustments have been made since it was established, but none have resulted in new counties.
