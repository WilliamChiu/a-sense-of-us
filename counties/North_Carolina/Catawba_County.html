Catawba County, North Carolina

In 2000, 20041 people in Catawba County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 119502 people in Catawba County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 1021 people in Camden County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 8942 people in Camden County, North Carolina lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1842 from Lincoln County. It was named for the Catawba tribe of Native Americans, who once inhabited the area. German Lutheran farmers settled the area in the 1700s.
