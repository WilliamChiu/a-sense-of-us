Pamlico County, North Carolina

In 2000, 1665 people in Pamlico County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10641 people in Pamlico County, North Carolina lived in family households.

In 2010, 3655 people in Northampton County, North Carolina lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 17692 people in Northampton County, North Carolina lived in family households.

It was named for Pamlico Sound, which adjoins it. Pamlico County remains rural in character and flavor, although the last decade has brought a good deal of residential development, largely the result of northern retirees and investors attracted to the many miles of waterfront property.
