Passaic County ( ) is a county located in the U.S. state of New Jersey. As of 2014, Passaic County's Census-estimated population was 508,856, an increase of 1.4% from the 2010 United States Census, when its population was enumerated at 501,226, and retaining its position as the state's ninth-most populous county. Its county seat is Paterson. It is part of the New York metropolitan area. The most populous place was Paterson, with 146,199 residents at the time of the 2010 Census, more than 29% of the county's population, while West Milford, covered 80.32 sqmi, the largest total area of any municipality, more than 40% of the county's area.
