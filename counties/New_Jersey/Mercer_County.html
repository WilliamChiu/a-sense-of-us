Mercer County, New Jersey

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 4.839349%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 49553 people in Mercer County, New Jersey lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 280116 people in Mercer County, New Jersey lived in family households.

In 2000, 21092 people in Mercer County, New Jersey lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 350761 people in Mercer County, New Jersey lived in the county.

In 2010, 4223 people in Gloucester County, New Jersey lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 288288 people in Gloucester County, New Jersey lived in the county.
