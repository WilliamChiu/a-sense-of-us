Bristol County, Massachusetts

In 2000, 77332 people in Bristol County, Massachusetts lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 443672 people in Bristol County, Massachusetts lived in family households.

In 2010, 152026 people in Baltimore city, Maryland lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 443736 people in Baltimore city, Maryland lived in family households.

Bristol County was created by the Plymouth Colony on June 2, 1685, and named after its "shire town" (county seat), Bristol. The Plymouth Colony merged into the Massachusetts Bay Colony in 1691.
