Mayes County, Oklahoma

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.3135562%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 4586 people in Mayes County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 33248 people in Mayes County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2000, 535 people in Mayes County, Oklahoma lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 38369 people in Mayes County, Oklahoma lived in the county.

In 2010, 3076 people in McIntosh County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 16831 people in McIntosh County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2010, 345 people in McIntosh County, Oklahoma lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 20252 people in McIntosh County, Oklahoma lived in the county.
