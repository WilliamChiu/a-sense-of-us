Cherokee County, Oklahoma

In 2000, 6359 people in Cherokee County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 34463 people in Cherokee County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2010, 3715 people in Caddo County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 23950 people in Caddo County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

According to a historian, Cherokee County was established in 1907. However, the ''Encyclopedia of Oklahoma History and Culture'', states that it was created from the Tahlequah District of the Cherokee Nation in 1906.
