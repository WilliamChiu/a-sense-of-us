Household Populations

Tulsa County, Oklahoma

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.96797943%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 95148 people in Tulsa County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 456063 people in Tulsa County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2000, 12088 people in Tulsa County, Oklahoma lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 563299 people in Tulsa County, Oklahoma lived in the county.

In 2010, 6276 people in Stephens County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 38232 people in Stephens County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2010, 540 people in Stephens County, Oklahoma lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 45048 people in Stephens County, Oklahoma lived in the county.

The history of Tulsa County greatly overlaps the history of the city of Tulsa. This section addresses events that largely occurred outside the present city limits of Tulsa.
