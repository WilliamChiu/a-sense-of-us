Oklahoma County, Oklahoma

In 2000, 115418 people in Oklahoma County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 528036 people in Oklahoma County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2010, 1647 people in Noble County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9641 people in Noble County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

Oklahoma County was originally called County Two and was one of seven counties established by the Organic Act of 1890.
