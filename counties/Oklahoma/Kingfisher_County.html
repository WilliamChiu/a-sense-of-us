Kingfisher County, Oklahoma

In 2000, 1507 people in Kingfisher County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 12144 people in Kingfisher County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2010, 972 people in Jefferson County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 5356 people in Jefferson County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

Limited archaeological surveys may have discovered evidence of pre-contact peoples, including Paleo-Indian and Archaic (6000 BC - 1 AD) groups that used the area for hunting and foraging. The historic Osage, Cheyenne, and Comanche tribes traversed the prairie grasslands of this area.
