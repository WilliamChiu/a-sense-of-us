Sequoyah County, Oklahoma

In 2000, 4348 people in Sequoyah County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 34134 people in Sequoyah County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

In 2010, 472 people in Roger Mills County, Oklahoma lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3164 people in Roger Mills County, Oklahoma lived in family households.

Archaeological sites within the borders of the present county date to the Archaic Period (6000 BC to 1 AD). A slightly smaller number of sites date to the Plains Village Period (1000 to 1500 AD).
