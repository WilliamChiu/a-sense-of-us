Lewis County, West Virginia

In 2000, 2471 people in Lewis County, West Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

Lewis County, founded in 1816, is a county located in the State of West Virginia. As of the 2010 census, the population was 16,372. Its county seat is Weston.
