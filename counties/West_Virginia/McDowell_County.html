McDowell County, West Virginia

In 2000, 3668 people in McDowell County, West Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 23358 people in McDowell County, West Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 2855 people in Lincoln County, West Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 18800 people in Lincoln County, West Virginia lived in family households.

McDowell County was formed by an act of the Virginia Legislature on February 20, 1858, from what was originally included in Tazewell County, Virginia.
