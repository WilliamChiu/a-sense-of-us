Marion County, West Virginia

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.96161366%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 9770 people in Marion County, West Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 45581 people in Marion County, West Virginia lived in family households.

In 2000, 1247 people in Marion County, West Virginia lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 56598 people in Marion County, West Virginia lived in the county.

In 2010, 4990 people in Logan County, West Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 31289 people in Logan County, West Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 464 people in Logan County, West Virginia lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 36743 people in Logan County, West Virginia lived in the county.
