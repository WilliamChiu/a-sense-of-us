Kootenai County, Idaho

In 2000, 15072 people in Kootenai County, Idaho lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 92213 people in Kootenai County, Idaho lived in family households.

In 2010, 2702 people in Idaho County, Idaho lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 13041 people in Idaho County, Idaho lived in family households.

Kootenai County was formed on December 22, 1864, from the northern section of Nez Perce County. It was named after the Kootenai, because it lies in the traditional area of settlement of the tribe.
