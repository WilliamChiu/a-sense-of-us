Walton County, Florida

In 2000, 6604 people in Walton County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 32202 people in Walton County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 1344 people in Union County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9413 people in Union County, Florida lived in family households.

Walton County was created in 1824. It was named for George Walton, Secretary of the Florida territory from 1821 to 1826.
