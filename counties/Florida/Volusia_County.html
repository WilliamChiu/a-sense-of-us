Volusia County, Florida

In 2000, 80799 people in Volusia County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 347807 people in Volusia County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 6136 people in Suwannee County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 34022 people in Suwannee County, Florida lived in family households.

Volusia County was named after the then-largest-community, Volusia, when the Florida legislature divided Orange County on December 29, 1854. At the time, Volusia County had as few as 600 residents.
