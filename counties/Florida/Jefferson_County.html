Jefferson County, Florida

In 2000, 1651 people in Jefferson County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10217 people in Jefferson County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 2681 people in Holmes County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 15514 people in Holmes County, Florida lived in family households.

Jefferson County was created in 1827. It was named for Thomas Jefferson, third President of the United States of America, who had died the year before the county's establishment.
