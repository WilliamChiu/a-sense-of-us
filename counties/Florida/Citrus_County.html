Citrus County, Florida

In 2000, 19327 people in Citrus County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 96597 people in Citrus County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 317921 people in Broward County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1413253 people in Broward County, Florida lived in family households.

Citrus County was first occupied about 10,000 years ago and settled about 2,500 years ago by mound-building Native Americans that built the complex that now forms the Crystal River Archeological Site. The site was occupied for about 2,000 years. Why the complex was abandoned is currently unknown.
