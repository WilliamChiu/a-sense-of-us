Madison County, Florida

In 2000, 2324 people in Madison County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14708 people in Madison County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 75657 people in Leon County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 184836 people in Leon County, Florida lived in family households.

Madison County was created in 1827. It was named for James Madison, fourth President of the United States of America, who served from 1809 to 1817.
