Lake County, Florida

In 2000, 31248 people in Lake County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 175513 people in Lake County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 6745 people in Jackson County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 35007 people in Jackson County, Florida lived in family households.

Lake County was created in 1887 from portions of Sumter County and Orange County. It was named for the many lakes contained within its borders.
