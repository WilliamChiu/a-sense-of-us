Gilchrist County, Florida

In 2000, 1634 people in Gilchrist County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11482 people in Gilchrist County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 14206 people in Flagler County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 80806 people in Flagler County, Florida lived in family households.

Gilchrist County was created in 1925 making it the youngest county in Florida. It was named for Albert W. Gilchrist, Governor of Florida from 1909 to 1913.
