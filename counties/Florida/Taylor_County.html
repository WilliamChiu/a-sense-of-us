Taylor County, Florida

In 2000, 2447 people in Taylor County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15592 people in Taylor County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 72275 people in Seminole County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 346928 people in Seminole County, Florida lived in family households.

Taylor County was created in 1856. It was named for Zachary Taylor, twelfth President of the United States of America, who served from 1849 to 1850.
