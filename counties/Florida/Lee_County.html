Lee County, Florida

In 2000, 76699 people in Lee County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 358572 people in Lee County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 2170 people in Jefferson County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 11252 people in Jefferson County, Florida lived in family households.

Lee County was created in 1887 from Monroe County. It was named for Robert E. Lee, Confederate general in the American Civil War.
