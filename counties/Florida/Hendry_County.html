Hendry County, Florida

In 2000, 4162 people in Hendry County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 29326 people in Hendry County, Florida lived in family households.

In 2010, 2141 people in Gulf County, Florida lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 10272 people in Gulf County, Florida lived in family households.

Hendry County was created in 1923. It was named for Major Francis A. Hendry, one of the first settlers.
