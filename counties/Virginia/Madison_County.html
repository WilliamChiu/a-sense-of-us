Madison County, Virginia

In 2000, 1433 people in Madison County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10868 people in Madison County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 31333 people in Loudoun County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 279806 people in Loudoun County, Virginia lived in family households.

Madison County was established in December 1792, created from Orange County. The county is named for the Madison family that owned land along the Rapidan River. President James Madison is a descendant of that family.
