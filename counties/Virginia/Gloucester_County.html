Gloucester County, Virginia

In 2000, 3958 people in Gloucester County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 30488 people in Gloucester County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 7994 people in Franklin County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 46693 people in Franklin County, Virginia lived in family households.

The written history of Gloucester County began soon after the English settlement of Jamestown in 1607. Before then, the area was inhabited for thousands of years by hunter-gatherer indigenous peoples; artifacts have been dated to at least the late Woodland Period.
