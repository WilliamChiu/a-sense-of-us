Henrico County, Virginia

In 2000, 46867 people in Henrico County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 211621 people in Henrico County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 1264 people in Greensville County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7452 people in Greensville County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 1611, Thomas Dale founded the Citie of Henricus on an island in the James River that is now called Farrar's Island. Henricus was named for Henry Frederick, Prince of Wales and was destroyed during the Indian massacre of 1622.
