Prince George County, Virginia

In 2000, 2451 people in Prince George County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 25628 people in Prince George County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 9030 people in Pittsylvania County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 53637 people in Pittsylvania County, Virginia lived in family households.

Prince George County was formed in 1703 in the Virginia Colony from a portion of Charles City County. It was named in honor of Prince George of Denmark, husband of Anne, Queen of Great Britain.
