York County, Virginia

In 2000, 5083 people in York County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 50587 people in York County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 2948 people in Westmoreland County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 14436 people in Westmoreland County, Virginia lived in family households.

The area which is now York County was long inhabited by succeeding cultures of Native Americans. These were hunter-gatherer groups during the late Woodland Period (1000 BC to AD 1000) and earlier.
