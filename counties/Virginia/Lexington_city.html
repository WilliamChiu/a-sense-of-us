Lexington city, Virginia

In 2000, 1570 people in Lexington city, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 17201 people in Harrisonburg city, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 24130 people in Harrisonburg city, Virginia lived in family households.

Lexington was named in 1778. It was one of the first of what would be many American places named after Lexington, Massachusetts, known for being the place at which the first shot was fired in the American Revolution.
