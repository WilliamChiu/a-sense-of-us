Covington city, Virginia

In 2000, 1259 people in Covington city, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5033 people in Covington city, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 24830 people in Chesapeake city, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 193658 people in Chesapeake city, Virginia lived in family households.

Covington is an independent city in the Commonwealth of Virginia. As of the 2010 census, the population was 5,961, making it the third-least populous city in Virginia. It is surrounded by Alleghany County, of which it is also the county seat. The Bureau of Economic Analysis combines the city of Covington with Alleghany county for statistical purposes.
