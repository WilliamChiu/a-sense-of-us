Warren County, Virginia

In 2000, 4389 people in Warren County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 26649 people in Warren County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 997 people in Surry County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 6061 people in Surry County, Virginia lived in family households.

Warren County was established in 1836 from Frederick and Shenandoah counties. The county is named for Joseph Warren. During the Civil War the Battle of Front Royal took place in the county on May 23, 1862.
