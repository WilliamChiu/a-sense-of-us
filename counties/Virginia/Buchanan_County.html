Buchanan County, Virginia

In 2000, 2881 people in Buchanan County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 22892 people in Buchanan County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 841 people in Bland County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 5297 people in Bland County, Virginia lived in family households.

The county was formed in 1858 from parts of Russell County and Tazewell County. It was named for James Buchanan, the 15th President of the United States. In 1880 the southwestern part of Buchanan County was combined with parts of Russell County and Wise County to become Dickenson County.
