Greene County, Virginia

In 2000, 1633 people in Greene County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 13464 people in Greene County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 4877 people in Gloucester County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 31613 people in Gloucester County, Virginia lived in family households.

Greene County was established in 1838 from Orange County. The county is named for American Revolutionary War hero Nathanael Greene.
