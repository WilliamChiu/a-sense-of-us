Albemarle County, Virginia

In 2000, 13452 people in Albemarle County, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 64249 people in Albemarle County, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 9849 people in Windham County, Vermont lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 33200 people in Windham County, Vermont lived in family households.

At the time of European encounter, the inhabitants of the area that became Albemarle County were a Siouan-speaking tribe called the Saponi.
