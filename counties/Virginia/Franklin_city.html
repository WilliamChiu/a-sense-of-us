Franklin city, Virginia

In 2000, 1253 people in Franklin city, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6819 people in Franklin city, Virginia lived in family households.

In 2010, 4088 people in Fairfax city, Virginia lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 17956 people in Fairfax city, Virginia lived in family households.

The city of Franklin had its beginnings in the 1830s as a railroad stop along the Blackwater River. During this era, the river was used to transport goods to and from the Albemarle Sound in North Carolina.
