Adair County, Iowa

In 2000, 1219 people in Adair County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6827 people in Adair County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 3655 people in Wells County, Indiana lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 23509 people in Wells County, Indiana lived in family households.

Adair County was formed in 1851 from sections of Pottawattamie County. It was named for John Adair, a general in the War of 1812, and the eighth Governor of Kentucky.
