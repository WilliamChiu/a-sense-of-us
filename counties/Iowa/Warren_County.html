Warren County, Iowa

In 2000, 4196 people in Warren County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 34760 people in Warren County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 2295 people in Union County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 9887 people in Union County, Iowa lived in family households.

Warren County was formed in 1846. It was named for General Joseph Warren, a hero in the American Revolutionary War. The present Warren County Court House was opened in 1939.
