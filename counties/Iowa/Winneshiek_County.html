Winneshiek County, Iowa

In 2000, 3119 people in Winneshiek County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15898 people in Winneshiek County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 1059 people in Wayne County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 5253 people in Wayne County, Iowa lived in family households.

The county was originally settled in 1848 in present day Bloomfield Township (near Castalia, Iowa) and in Washington Township (near Fort Atkinson, Iowa). It was organized in 1847, and named after a chief of the Winnebago tribe.
