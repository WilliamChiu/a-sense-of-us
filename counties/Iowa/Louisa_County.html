Louisa County, Iowa

In 2000, 1449 people in Louisa County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10567 people in Louisa County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 2575 people in Kossuth County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 12711 people in Kossuth County, Iowa lived in family households.

Louisa County was formed on December 7, 1836. It was named after Louisa Massey, who was very well known here at the time because she avenged the murder of her brother when she shot the party responsible.
