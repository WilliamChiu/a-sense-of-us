Sioux County, Iowa

In 2000, 2980 people in Sioux County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 25961 people in Sioux County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 1786 people in Sac County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 8341 people in Sac County, Iowa lived in family households.

Sioux County was formed on January 15, 1851. It has been self-governed since January 20, 1860. It was named after the Sioux tribe.
