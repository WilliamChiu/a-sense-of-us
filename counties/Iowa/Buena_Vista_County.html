Buena Vista County, Iowa

In 2000, 2898 people in Buena Vista County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 16132 people in Buena Vista County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 4413 people in Boone County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 21125 people in Boone County, Iowa lived in family households.

Buena Vista County was formed on January 15, 1851 from open land. It was named in memory of the Battle of Buena Vista in the Mexican-American War.
