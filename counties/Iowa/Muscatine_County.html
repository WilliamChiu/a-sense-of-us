Muscatine County, Iowa

In 2000, 5473 people in Muscatine County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 35525 people in Muscatine County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 1766 people in Monona County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7277 people in Monona County, Iowa lived in family households.

Muscatine County was formed on December 7, 1836. One history of the county claims it was named for Muscatine Island in the Mississippi River.
