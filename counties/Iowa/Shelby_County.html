Shelby County, Iowa

In 2000, 1671 people in Shelby County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11203 people in Shelby County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 800 people in Ringgold County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 4152 people in Ringgold County, Iowa lived in family households.

Shelby County was formed on January 15, 1851. It was named after General Isaac Shelby, a hero in the American Revolutionary War and the first Governor of Kentucky.
