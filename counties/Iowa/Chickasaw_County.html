Chickasaw County, Iowa

In 2000, 1772 people in Chickasaw County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11098 people in Chickasaw County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 2759 people in Cedar County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 15416 people in Cedar County, Iowa lived in family households.

Chickasaw County was founded on January 15, 1851. It was named after the Chickasaw tribe, which lived in the Southern United States at the time.
