Wayne County, Iowa

In 2000, 978 people in Wayne County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5620 people in Wayne County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 6314 people in Wapello County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 28444 people in Wapello County, Iowa lived in family households.

Wayne County was formed in 1846 but was still attached to other counties for governmental purposes. It was named after General Anthony Wayne.
