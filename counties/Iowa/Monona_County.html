Monona County, Iowa

In 2000, 1676 people in Monona County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 8071 people in Monona County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 6169 people in Marshall County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 33104 people in Marshall County, Iowa lived in family households.

Monona County was formed in 1851. It was perhaps named for the Indian word meaning "beautiful valley."
