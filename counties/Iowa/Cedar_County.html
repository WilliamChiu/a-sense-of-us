Cedar County, Iowa

In 2000, 2395 people in Cedar County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 15509 people in Cedar County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 1605 people in Calhoun County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 7827 people in Calhoun County, Iowa lived in family households.

Cedar County was formed on December 21, 1837, from sections of Dubuque County. It was named for the Cedar River.
