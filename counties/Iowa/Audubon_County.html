Audubon County, Iowa

In 2000, 924 people in Audubon County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 5726 people in Audubon County, Iowa lived in family households.

In 2010, 692 people in Adams County, Iowa lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 3223 people in Adams County, Iowa lived in family households.

Audubon County was formed on January 15, 1851 from sections of Pottawattamie County. It was named after John James Audubon. The current Audubon County Court House was opened in 1940.
