Eddy County, New Mexico

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.7103503%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 44782 people in Eddy County, New Mexico lived in family households.

In 2000, 750 people in Eddy County, New Mexico lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 51658 people in Eddy County, New Mexico lived in the county.
