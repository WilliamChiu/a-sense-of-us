De Baca County is a county located in the U.S. state of New Mexico. As of the 2010 census, the population was 2,022, making it the second-least populous county in New Mexico. Its county seat is Fort Sumner. The county is named for Ezequiel Cabeza De Baca, the second elected Governor of New Mexico.
