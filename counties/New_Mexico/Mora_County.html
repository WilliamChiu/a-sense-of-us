Mora County, New Mexico

In 2000, 702 people in Mora County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 4422 people in Mora County, New Mexico lived in family households.

In 2010, 2795 people in Los Alamos County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 15061 people in Los Alamos County, New Mexico lived in family households.

Prior to Spanish conquest, the Mora area was Indian country. Although not an area of heavy Indian settlement by such tribes as the Pueblo Indians, the Mora valley was much used by nomadic tribes: the Utes, Navajos and Apache.
