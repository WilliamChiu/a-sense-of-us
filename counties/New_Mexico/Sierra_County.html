Sierra County, New Mexico

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.6691575%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 2844 people in Sierra County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10160 people in Sierra County, New Mexico lived in family households.

In 2000, 266 people in Sierra County, New Mexico lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 13270 people in Sierra County, New Mexico lived in the county.

In 2010, 14672 people in San Juan County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 1754 people in San Juan County, New Mexico lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 130044 people in San Juan County, New Mexico lived in the county.

Sierra County is a county located in the U.S. state of New Mexico. As of the 2010 census, the population was 11,988. Its county seat is Truth or Consequences.
