Catron County, New Mexico

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.9887636%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 622 people in Catron County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 2911 people in Catron County, New Mexico lived in family households.

In 2000, 10 people in Catron County, New Mexico lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 3543 people in Catron County, New Mexico lived in the county.

In 2010, 536499 people in Union County, New Jersey lived in the county.
