Valencia County, New Mexico

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.6592035%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 6686 people in Valencia County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 58095 people in Valencia County, New Mexico lived in family households.

In 2000, 1371 people in Valencia County, New Mexico lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 66152 people in Valencia County, New Mexico lived in the county.

In 2010, 7543 people in Taos County, New Mexico lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 24924 people in Taos County, New Mexico lived in family households.

In 2010, 470 people in Taos County, New Mexico lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 32937 people in Taos County, New Mexico lived in the county.

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 1068 sqmi, of which 1066 sqmi is land and 2.1 sqmi (0.2%) is water. It is the second-smallest county in New Mexico by area.
