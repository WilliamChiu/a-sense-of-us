Montgomery County, Missouri

In 2000, 1670 people in Montgomery County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 10143 people in Montgomery County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2010, 1992 people in Mississippi County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 10492 people in Mississippi County, Missouri lived in family households.

The county has evidence of human habitation from 10,000 years ago, the Archaic period of indigenous Americans. An ancient site was found during archaeological excavations at Graham Cave on the Loutre River.
