Andrew County, Missouri

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 0.38434267%. This change is reasionably similar to that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 1926 people in Andrew County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 14302 people in Andrew County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2000, 264 people in Andrew County, Missouri lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 16492 people in Andrew County, Missouri lived in the county.
