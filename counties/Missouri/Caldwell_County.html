Caldwell County, Missouri

In 2000, 1160 people in Caldwell County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 7690 people in Caldwell County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2010, 42737 people in Boone County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 110907 people in Boone County, Missouri lived in family households.

Caldwell County was originally part of Ray County. The first white settler was Jesse Mann, Sr., who settled one-half mile northeast of the public square of Kingston on Shoal Creek in 1831. The early settlers moved back south in 1832 for better protection during the Black Hawk War uprising.
