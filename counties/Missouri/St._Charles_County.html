St. Charles County, Missouri

In 2000, 30570 people in St. Charles County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 249525 people in St. Charles County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2010, 2830 people in Ray County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20353 people in Ray County, Missouri lived in family households.

The County of St. Charles was originally called the District of St. Charles and had no definite limits until 1816 to 1818 when neighboring counties were formed. The borders of St. Charles are the same today as they were in 1818.
