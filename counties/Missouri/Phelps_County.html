Phelps County, Missouri

In 2000, 6849 people in Phelps County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 30544 people in Phelps County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2010, 2932 people in Pemiscot County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 15162 people in Pemiscot County, Missouri lived in family households.

The first settlers came to this area in 1818 building along the riverbanks, doing a little farming. In 1844 John Webber built the first house within the present city limits of Rolla.
