Callaway County, Missouri

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by 4.8189282%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region's economic circumstance as well as other variables helped to increase the number of household-population.

In 2000, 5080 people in Callaway County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 31852 people in Callaway County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2000, 3834 people in Callaway County, Missouri lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 40766 people in Callaway County, Missouri lived in the county.
