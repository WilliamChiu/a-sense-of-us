Mississippi County, Missouri

In 2000, 1937 people in Mississippi County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 11220 people in Mississippi County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2010, 4652 people in Marion County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 22761 people in Marion County, Missouri lived in family households.

Mississippi County is located in what was formerly known as "Tywappity Bottom," a vast floodplain area bordered by the Scott County Hills on the north, St. James Bayou on the south, the Mississippi River on the east and Little River on the west.
