Daviess County, Missouri

From 2000 to 2010, the number of people living in households has changed by -0.6502867%. This change is a drastic departure from that of the nation. Data from this state suggests that the region suffered stagnated household-population growth perhaps as a result of regional economic issues.

In 2000, 1019 people in Daviess County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2000, 6941 people in Daviess County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2000, 56 people in Daviess County, Missouri lived in group quarters.

In 2000, 8016 people in Daviess County, Missouri lived in the county.

In 2010, 3763 people in Crawford County, Missouri lived in nonfamily households.

In 2010, 20601 people in Crawford County, Missouri lived in family households.

In 2010, 332 people in Crawford County, Missouri lived in group quarters.

In 2010, 24696 people in Crawford County, Missouri lived in the county.
